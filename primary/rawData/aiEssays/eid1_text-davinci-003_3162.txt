

Dear Editor:

I am writing to express my opinion on the effects computers have on people. 

In this day and age, it seems almost inconceivable to go a single day without using a computer in some way. We turn to computers to research, shop, communicate, and entertain. As computers become increasingly integrated into our lives, it is important to consider the potential effects they may have on people – both positive and negative. 

There are many areas in which computers can have a positive impact. For example, computers make it easier for us to access vast amounts of information, uncovering discoveries and insights that weren’t previously possible. Businesses are also able to streamline processes and increase efficiency by using computer technology. In addition, computers can simplify and improve communication, making it easier for people to stay connected, even if they are on opposite ends of the globe.

At the same time, excessive use of computers can be detrimental to people. Spending too much time on computers can lead to physical strains, such as sore eyes or fatigue, as well as mental fatigue.
