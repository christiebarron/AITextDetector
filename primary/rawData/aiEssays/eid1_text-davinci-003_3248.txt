

Dear Editor:

As a member of the community, I am writing to share my opinion about the effects computers have on people.

Today, we are becoming increasingly reliant on computers. The way technology has developed has drastically impacted the way people communicate, learn, shop, and interact with others. Computers have revolutionized the way we do countless tasks, from providing entertainment, to online banking, to conducting research.

However, while computers are undoubtedly useful, they can also be detrimental to our health and well-being. Spending too much time in front of a computer can have detrimental physical effects, such as neck and back pain or carpal tunnel syndrome. It can also lead to a lack of physical activity, weakening our muscles and bones over time.

In addition, too much computing can also lead to social and emotional problems. It can reduce our ability to communicate face-to-face, decrease our ability to think critically, and increase anxiety and depression.

Therefore, I believe it is important to recognize and understand the effects computers can have on our lives. We should be aware of the potential risks and take the necessary precautions to protect ourselves from potential harm.

Thank you for your time and consideration
