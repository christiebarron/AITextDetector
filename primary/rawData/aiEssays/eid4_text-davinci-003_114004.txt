

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves as a reminder that despite present challenges, there is hope for the future. By ending with this sentiment,the author emphasizes the protagonist's determination and courage to keep trying and to never give up, even when faced with the seemingly impossible. The image of the hibiscus blooming in the spring serves as a symbol for the protagonist's journey to success and the importance of persisting even in the face of hardship.
