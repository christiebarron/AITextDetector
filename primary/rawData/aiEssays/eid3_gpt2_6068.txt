Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways. First of all the setting allows him to cycle or ride the roads without needing to change on a daily basis. As far as he is concerned his ability to ride these roadways will come down to preference in the shortterm. There is a good chance he does get hit by emanated lights or other road hazards, which increases risk of collisions and the amount of risk of a crash.
