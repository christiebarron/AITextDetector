

The ending of "Winter Hibiscus" by Minfong Ho serves to offer a message of hopefulness and resilience to the reader. The narrator takes solace in a symbol of determination and perseverance—the winter hibiscus—and its ability to survive winter's harshness until springtime. The narrator expresses that they too are capable of surviving difficult experiences, offering hope for the future. The message of the text is that even in the toughest times and situations, there is always hope for a brighter tomorrow.
