

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho expresses the idea of resilience and resilience in the face of failure. The main character is struggling to pass a difficult school test and is beginning to doubt her own worth and capabilities. Through the story, she is reminded of the determination and resilience of the hibiscus bush outside her window that manages to bloom in the midst of winter despite the cold weather. The ending serves to motivate her to keep trying and to persevere, knowing that when spring comes and the hibiscus buds again, she too can try again and make it through the test.
