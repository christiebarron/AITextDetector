The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' is that she had no choice but to make the choice now: to follow the path that, she had already started.

"She is living the ideal life, she is living for a new life, of 'better' and 'better things'," she says of how the family moved into the home. "I am not thinking I got over [her] feeling. If you are not going to give up on yourself, we will. We will go for the journey, for the road and for the road is for you.''
