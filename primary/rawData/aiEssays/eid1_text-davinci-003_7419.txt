

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves as an affirmation of the protagonist's resilience. The protagonist experienced deep sadness, frustration, and pain during the winter; yet, despite feeling those negative and overwhelming emotions, they are determined to take the test again when the snows melt and the geese return. This ending serves as an empowering reminder that regardless of the obstacles one encounters, it is possible to take another attempt at a goal and that failure is not an end-all. Ultimately, the ending is a hopeful sign of the protagonist's strength, courage, and determination.
