

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves to give the protagonist hope for the future. Despite her struggles and failures, she has the optimism that in the spring, with the return of the birds, she will be able to take the same difficult test again and be successful. It is a reminder of the cyclical nature of life and the beauty of its possibilities. The hopefulness reflects the idea that, with the right effort, one can overcome adversity and reemerge triumphant.
