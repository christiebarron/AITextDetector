

The conclusion of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves to reinforce the idea of hope and resilience. The last line expresses an anticipation for the arrival of spring and a renewal of hope that the protagonist will have another chance to take the test when the winter is gone. It is a reminder that fresh starts are possible and that life is cyclical with the beauty of nature providing a constant source of comfort. The ending of the story is also a reminder that despite hardship and pain, we can still find hope in the future.
