

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves to draw a parallel between the character's test-taking journey and the growth of the hibiscus, symbolizing hope and resilience. The closing lines refer to the character's determination to go back and take the test again in the spring, despite her previous failure. This implies that just like the hibiscus that will bloom in the spring despite the cold winter, the character will be able to overcome any obstacles she may face and reach her goal despite the odds. The ending reflects the themes of perseverance and resilience that are present throughout the story, and it suggests that with dedication and hard work, anyone can achieve their goals.
