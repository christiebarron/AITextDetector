

The ending of this story reflects a hopeful optimism on the part of the protagonist. The protagonist has just failed a challenging test, and is uncertain what the future will bring, but still has faith that things will go better in the future. The image of the flowering hibiscus in the springtime symbolizes the idea of hope: that the protagonist will have the opportunity to take the test again, and that things will go better for them this time. The hope of the blooming hibiscus serves as a reminder that no matter how difficult things may look in the present, things can and will get better in the future.
