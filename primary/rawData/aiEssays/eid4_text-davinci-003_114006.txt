

The author purposefully ends Winter Hibiscus with an uplifting message of hope and determination, showing the reader that no matter how challenging life may be, there is always a chance to try again. The image of the hibiscus budding in the spring serves as a metaphor for the protagonist: no matter how difficult the winter may seem, they will be able to bloom and grow during the coming seasons. The narrator is choosing to focus on the possibilities of the future, not the disappointments of the past.
