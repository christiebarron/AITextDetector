

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with these words in order to emphasize the cyclical nature of the seasons and how they shape much of our lives. The narrator of the story is about to take a test, representing a challenge that will shape her future. She takes comfort in the fact that the possibilities before her are just like the blooming of the hibiscus each spring after the snows melt and the geese return. No matter what happens, the cycle of life will continue, and when she is ready to face the challenge again, the hibiscus will be there waiting for her.
