

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this question because it ties the entire poem together. The speaker of the poem has contemplated on the struggles of life, but finds solace in the knowledge that things can get better. The image of the hibiscus returning in the spring and the speaker redoing their test is symbolic of this hope for the future. It's an optimistic way to close the poem, which emphasizes that struggles can be faced with resilience.
