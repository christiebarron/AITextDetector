

By ending the poem with this thought, Minfong Ho hints at resilience and hope. The speaker is thinking of the future, when the cold and snow has gone and nature is teeming once more. This gives the speaker a sense of comfort and acknowledgement that although the cold and hard winter will pass, and life will go on. In the same way, the speaker is willing to face the test again, this time with a sense of optimism and hope. This serves to offer readers a glimpse of positivity in a seemingly gloomy and despairing situation.
