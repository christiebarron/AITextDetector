

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho offers hope and reassurance. It reminds readers that even in the dark of winter, spring will follow, and new growth and opportunity will emerge from the struggle. The decision to pursue higher education is an important step, but it's also a scary one. The narrator is reassuring herself that when the flowers and geese return in the spring, she can renew her attempt to take the test. This ending provides closure to the narrative while offering reassurance to the narrator and readers in uncertain times.
