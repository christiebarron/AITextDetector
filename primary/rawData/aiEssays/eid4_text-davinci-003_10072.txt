

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this thought to emphasize the growth and development of the main character, Ahn. By having Ahn think about her next test in the spring, she is showing that she has grown and gained more knowledge from her experiences and studies throughout the story. In addition, the mention of the 'snows melting and the geese return' symbolize the passing of winter and the arrival of spring, suggesting new beginnings and fresh starts - a central theme in this story.
