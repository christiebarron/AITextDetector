

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho conveys a message of hope and resilience. By tying the protagonist's success in her exams to the return of nature's signs of spring, the author conveys the idea that with hope and patience, difficult times will come to an end. The protagonist is expressing her inner determination to overcome her challenges by connecting her success to the rebirth of nature every spring. The ending of the story is a reminder that things can get better and that there is always hope to improve one's situation.
