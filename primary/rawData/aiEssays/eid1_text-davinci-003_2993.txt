I recently read an article in the newspaper that questioned the impact of computers on our lives and asked whether or not they are a positive development. After careful consideration, I believe that computers have had a largely positive effect on society, and I urge readers to consider this opinion when forming their own.

Computers have revolutionized how we consume and create knowledge. They allow us to access information faster and more accurately than ever before. This, in turn, has had a profound effect on the way we learn and interact with the world. Not only that, but computers have enabled us to become more efficient and effective in our work and leisure activities. For example, there are countless apps and services that enable us to quickly research, analyze, and communicate key ideas.

Furthermore, computers have advanced the opportunities available to businesses and individuals. They enable people to work from home, collaborate across long distances, and access a greater range of services and products than ever before. This opens up countless opportunities for social and economic progress.
