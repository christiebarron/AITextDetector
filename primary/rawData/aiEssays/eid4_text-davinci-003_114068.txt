

The author wanted to depict hope and renewal with the ending of the poem. The mention of the geese returning symbolizes the return of life and the idea that spring is just around the corner, with its promise of new beginnings and healing. The hibiscus blooming again brings a comfort to the speaker, as does the idea of taking the test again in the future. This shows that despite any setbacks, there is the potential for a brighter future, and the hope that the speaker can overcome their struggles.
