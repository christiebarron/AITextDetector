

This line is meant to signify a time of renewal and hope. Through the winter season, Minfong Ho reflects on the struggles she faced and the strength she found within herself. The return of the geese symbolizes the start of a new season and a new beginning, one filled with optimism and potential. Minfong Ho knows that this new beginning is an ideal opportunity for her to try again, to prove that she is better prepared and to eventually succeed. Her hope is that within this new season, her dreams of finally succeeding will be realized.
