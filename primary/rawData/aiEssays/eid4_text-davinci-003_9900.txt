

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this description to express hope and faith. She uses the symbolism of snow melting, geese returning, and the hibiscus budding to represent growth, new beginnings, and resilience. The protagonist is apprehensive about her school entrance exam, but she is confident that with spring comes hope and she will be successful on her second attempt. The line emphasizes that with the help of nature, and a positive attitude, anything is possible.
