

The ending of Winter Hibiscus symbolizes the resilience of the protagonist, Ping. She found comfort and strength in the small, red hibiscus that she was able to nurture despite the cold winter conditions. Therefore, even though she had failed her math exam, she is determined to persevere and take it again in the spring when the snows melt, symbolizing a new beginning. It suggests that the hibiscus serves as a reminder to Ping to stay strong and try again.
