

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is symbolic of hope and renewal. The hibiscus is a symbol of the main character's determination to take the college entrance exam again in the spring. By ending with the main character vowing to retake the test when the hibiscus is in bloom, the author suggests that difficult times can pass and renewal is possible. The imagery of the returning geese and melting snow point to a brighter future. The ending offers a glimmer of hope after a hard winter and reflects the main character's determination and resilience.
