Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho ends with this hopeful statement because it emphasizes the cyclical and hopeful nature of life. The protagonist is facing uncertainty and dread, but is comforted by the knowledge that there will be an opportunity for change in the future when the snows melt and the geese return.
