Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways.


(To see the details of the setting effect with the different settings and apply them using either the ROTC or the GSM app)

On the GSM apps you can select the "Good Day to Bike" mode as the driver.

On the "Good Day to Bike" mode, cyclists of four days or more can choose between a cycling mode which enables you to enter the car and a cycling mode which enables you to walk or cycle.

For ainois and its sister city of New York, the riding time is 6 - 15 minutes for regular cyclists.

On a GSM app you can choose the "Good Morning" setting and choose cycling mode in the mode selector.
