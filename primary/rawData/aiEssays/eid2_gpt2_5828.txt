The government would also like to highlight how in 2015, the International Library Association (ILBA) proposed that some library books be censored within two years, due to the rise of online book censorship that has been widely welcomed by book creators. The report was leaked to the press on October 9 and is expected to generate a global controversy after being published in The Times and other publications in China.
