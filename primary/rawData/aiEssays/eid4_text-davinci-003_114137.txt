

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho emphasizes the resilience and strength of the protagonist. After facing many hardships this winter, the narrator chooses to see the ending of winter as something of an opportunity to start fresh and to prepare for something new. By taking her test again, she is taking control of her own destiny and is choosing a positive attitude in facing the challenges this new season will bring. With her strong will and determination, the narrator is hoping to find success and happiness in the future. The hopeful message that comes from the ending reflects the narrator's inner strength and provides a sense of empowerment.
