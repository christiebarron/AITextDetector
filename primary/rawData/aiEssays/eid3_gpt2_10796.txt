Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways. A lot of these values are different in different countries Specifications and methods for determining the quality of the tyre (in relation to weather, performance, traction control and braking conditions, etc.) must be taken into account when choosing a tyre if the road is 'Rough Road Ahead' is the 'normal' value.

If the road is 'Rough Road ravaged' or 'Rough Road Ahead' and you choose the normal tyre, the 'normal' weight for the 'road' is 1.05m (100) (Gross weight will change to 150kg (250lbs) in some instances when 'Rough Road Ahead' is applied to a high-carbon road and tyres are lighter when this is applied to other types of road).
