

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is meant to demonstrate the idea of perseverance and determination, even in the face of difficult odds. The protagonist has been struggling to adequately prepare for a very important exam due to her various responsibilities and obligations. Despite feeling overwhelmed and discouraged by this huge task, the protagonist is still determined to keep trying. The ending symbolizes her decision to never give up and to focus on her goals. She acknowledges the difficulty of the situation and decides that when things are more ideal in the spring – when all of nature seems to be reawakening – she will take the test again. This signifies her courage and her drive to succeed.
