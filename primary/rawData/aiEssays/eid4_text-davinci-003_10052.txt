

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho reflects the protagonist's hope in the face of an uncertain future. She has failed her Science test and fears that she will have to take it again in the Spring. Yet, as she watches a hibiscus flower bravely open in the winter, she is filled with optimism and resolves to take the test again if she can reach the spring. By connecting the protagonist's challenge to the resilience of the hibiscus, Minfong Ho provides an uplifting conclusion to the story that celebrates the power of hope in the face of adversity.
