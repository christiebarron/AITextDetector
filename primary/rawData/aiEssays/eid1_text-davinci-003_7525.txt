

This line serves as an expression of hope. Despite the difficult passage from winter to spring, with its cold weather and melting snow, Ho conveys optimism in the face of adversity. At the end of the poem, she expresses the belief that eventually, the cold season will pass, and things will get better; just as hope will come in the form of the spring, she will take the test again and be successful. In this way, the ending of the poem symbolically reflects the struggles, successes, and hopes of the main character.
