

Dear Editor,

As someone who has grown up with technology, I’ve seen firsthand the power of computers and the effects they can have on people today. It’s nearly impossible to ignore the presence of computers in our everyday lives. They’ve become an integral part of our culture, and there are both positive and negative effects to consider. 

On the one hand, computers have helped us accomplish things faster and more efficiently. We’re able to communicate instantaneously with people across the globe and access enormous amounts of information in a fraction of the time it would take without them. In the workplace, computers help us store and organize data in ways we could never do manually.

On the other hand, computers can create distractions that keep us from connecting with one another and appreciating the world around us. We can become so engrossed in our devices that we forget to look up and notice the beauty of the real world.
