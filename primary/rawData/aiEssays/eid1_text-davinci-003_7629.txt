

The ending of Winter Hibiscus highlights an important theme in the story, which is the idea of resilience and renewal. The protagonist has experienced great difficulty throughout the winter, but she is determined to remain resilient and try again the following spring. The imagery of a hibiscus budding in the spring reflects this idea of renewal and hope for a brighter future. The protagonist decides to take the test again in the spring to show that she will not be defeated by any obstacle, and that she will keep on trying until she succeeds. Theauthor leaves the reader with a message of hope, that with resilience and determination even difficult circumstances can be overcome.
