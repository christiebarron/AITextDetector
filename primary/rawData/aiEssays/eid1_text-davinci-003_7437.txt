

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho provides a sense of hope. The narrator is looking forward to the coming of spring when the snow melts, the geese return, and the hibiscus buds, a symbol of perseverance and hope. The narrator is determined to retake the test and looking to the winter hibiscus for strength and encouragement. The use of the imagery of the hibiscus gives the ending a sense of optimism as the narrator finds comfort in the hibiscus and its promise of renewal in the spring. The ending of Winter Hibiscus conveys the hope of second chances and the promise of a brighter future.
