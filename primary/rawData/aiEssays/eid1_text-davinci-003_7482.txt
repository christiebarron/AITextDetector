

The ending of this poem speaks to the idea of resilience. Despite the cold winter, the hibiscus (a flower) miraculously survives the harsh winter and returns in the spring. In the same way, Minfong Ho uses the metaphor of the hibiscus to express the idea of resilience. By comparing the hibiscus surviving the harsh winter to her taking the test again, she implies that no matter how hard a situation is, it can be overcome and that there is hope in the face of difficult times. The poem speaks to the idea of hope and resilience, and so it ends on an uplifting note.
