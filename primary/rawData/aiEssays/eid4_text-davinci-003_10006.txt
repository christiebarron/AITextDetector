This serves as a reminder that all of us face moments of trepidation in our lives, but that our resilience and optimism will help us to overcome these obstacles. By connecting the passage of the seasons with the idea of starting anew, this line conveys a timeless notion of hope and growth. By ending the poem in this way, Minfong Ho is leaving the reader with a message of optimism and resilience in the face of difficulty.
