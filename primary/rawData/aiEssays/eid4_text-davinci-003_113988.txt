

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho emphasizes how far the narrator has come in her life journey. The narrator has gone from a naive and fearful young girl to a young woman who is confident in her own capability. She has embraced the harshness of the winter weather, and looks forward to the coming of spring. The line also foreshadows the also journey of the narrator taking a seemingly difficult test, which she is now prepared to face with strength and determination. The line serves as a reminder that despite the hardship and obstacles that life may present, there is always hope for a brighter future.
