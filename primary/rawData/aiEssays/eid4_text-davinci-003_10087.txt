

This ending is symbolic of how the narrator is learning to let go of her fear and how even if she fails the test this time she will have a new opportunity in the spring. The return of the geese, the melting snow, and the budding of the hibiscus are all symbols of new beginnings and the cycle of life. By ending with this hopeful description, the narrator is reflecting on the idea that even if something doesn't work out in the present, it may work out in the future.
