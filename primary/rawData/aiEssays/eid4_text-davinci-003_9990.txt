

The fact that the hibiscus buds in the spring represents new beginnings for the protagonist and her sister. The protagonist is determined to retake the test in the spring, which represents her dedication and determination to get a better outcome. This scene serves as an uplifting and encouraging conclusion to the story that gives the audience hope in the resolution of the protagonist's predicament. The renewal of the hibiscus is a symbol of resilience, growth, and progress that speaks to the main themes of the story: perseverance and determination.
