

Dear Editor,

I am writing to share my thoughts about the effects of computers on people.

Over the past few decades, computers have become an integral part of our lives. They have revolutionized how we work, store information, and communicate. Computers have changed the way people interact, creating a more interconnected world that helps to break down barriers of language, culture, and geography.

As with most advancements, there are both positive and negative aspects to consider when it comes to computers. On the one hand, they have made life easier in many ways. They have enabled us to work more efficiently, store greater amounts of data, and access information quickly. On the other hand, there is a risk that people will become overly dependent on computers and the technology that comes with them.

That being said, I believe that the positive aspects of computers far outweigh the drawbacks. For example, computers have made it easier to get an education with online classes, increased access to the workplace for people with disabilities, and allowed for more collaboration across the globe.
