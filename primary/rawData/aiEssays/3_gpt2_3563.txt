Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways.

First of all, it increases the chances and opportunities that a rider will be involved in a collision with someone who is not a member of the general public. Secondly, it increases the chances that a rider might be unable to intervene with the person causing the collision.

If you have an ongoing cycle accident with other cyclists and you notice any serious physical/emotional issues, you are more likely to have to intervene. It is more important now to speak to your insurance company to ensure people understand.

To protect you from an accident from an accident that may occur, please talk to your car insurance company for advice about ensuring you are well insured.
