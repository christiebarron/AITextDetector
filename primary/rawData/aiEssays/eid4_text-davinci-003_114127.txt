

This ending is a symbolic way of indicating how even though an individual might feel overwhelmed by life's challenges, hope and courage will enable them to face such challenges in the future. The hope that the hibiscus will be budding again in the spring serves as a reminder of the possibility of change and growth, which is something the protagonist realizes and intends to take advantage of. In this way, the ending encapsulates the idea that the protagonist will eventually be able to achieve success and take the test again.
