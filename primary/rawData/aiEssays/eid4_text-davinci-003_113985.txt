

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this question in order to emphasize the importance of education and perseverance. She shows us that even with difficult circumstances, the protagonist is determined to find a way to achieve and reach her dreams. Furthermore, the reference to the hibiscus symbolizes both hope and resilience. It is a reminder that there will be a chance for renewal and to make a fresh start. By ending with this question, the author also reminds her readers that even when faced with challenges our goals can be achieved with the right attitude and if we don't give up.
