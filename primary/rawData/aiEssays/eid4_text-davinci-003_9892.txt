

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is a hopeful and uplifting one, nourishing the idea of growth and renewal. It is a reminder that even in the hardest of times, difficult things can eventually turn around. The protagonist has gone through a difficult experience taking a difficult test, and though they may have not obtained the desired results they are still determined to go on and pursue their dreams and passions, and find success down the road. The closing line looks forward to the spring and growth, showing that anything is possible, even after a hard time.
