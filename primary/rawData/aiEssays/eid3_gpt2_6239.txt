Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways. The cyclist can now take the bike home in different lengths depending on what kind of tyre and brake pedal they use. However the set of colours and the settings do not change.

The set of colours include light grey, light white and gray. The setting does not alter the colour of the brake pedal.

A rider can set the setting by clicking the gear icon in the upper right-hand corner of the saddle in the 'Rough Road Ahead' Settings menu.
