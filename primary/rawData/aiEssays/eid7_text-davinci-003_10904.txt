

I remember one time when I was out grocery shopping and the checkout line was incredibly long. Instead of getting frustrated, I decided to stay patient. I reminded myself that everyone had places to be and it would only take a few minutes to get through the line. As the line slowly inched forward, I used the time to chat with the people around me. We talked about our grocery lists, current events, and everyday life. We even shared some jokes!

Before I knew it, my turn had come to check out. Although it had taken a while, I had made the most of the situation and was in a better mood than when I arrived. To me, staying patient can really pay off when it's done in the right spirit.
