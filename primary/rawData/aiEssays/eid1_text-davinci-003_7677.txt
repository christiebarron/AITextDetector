

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this reflective imagery to symbolize the protagonist's perseverance as she prepares to take her college admissions exam a second time. This imagery highlights the importance of resilience and hope in the face of adversity. The protagonist's choice to take the exam again in the spring symbolizes her strength and determination to achieve success even in difficult circumstances. Additionally, the imagery of the snows melting and the return of the geese can also be seen to represent a figurative rebirth, as if the protagonist has cleared a hurdle and is poised for spring and returning growth.
