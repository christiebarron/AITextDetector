The winter hibiscus symbolizes the struggle that the protagonist is going through: they are feeling powerless and fear has taken over, but they will have renewed courage when the geese return and the hibiscus buds again, suggesting that the future is filled with hope. This ending reflects a theme of resilience and perseverance, as the protagonist finds the courage to take the test again when spring arrives.
