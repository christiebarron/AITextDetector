

The ending of this poem offers hope in facing difficult challenges. Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho speaks of a young student whose family is struggling financially and academically. The student is taking an important test and is feeling overwhelmed. However, the poem ends with the student in a place of hope that when the warm season returns and the snows melt, the geese return and the hibiscus blooms, they will take the test again and pass. This ending narrative provides the reader with an inspiring message of resilience and strength.
