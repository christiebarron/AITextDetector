

The narrator of the story is a young girl who has been studying for a big exam she needs to take at school. At the end of the story when she has been preparing herself mentally for the exam, she reflects on the winter hibiscus growing in her family's garden and how it reminds her of her family's strength and resilience. The ending of the story reflects her hopefulness that she will pass the exam. She has studied hard and the symbolic winter hibiscus will come back in the spring, reflecting her own resilience and reminding her that she can persevere through difficult times. The ending of the story symbolically links the return of the winter hibiscus to her own growth and perseverance and her desire to show that she can pass the exam.
