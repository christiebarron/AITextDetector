

Minfong Ho ends the story Winter Hibiscus with this line to illustrate the main character's resilience and determination. The main character, Ahn-yi, was worried about an upcoming exam test, and the final line implies that despite her worries, she will take the test and have the courage to face her fears. By linking the test to the imagery of spring and new life, Minfong Ho is also conveying the hope that Ahn-yi will overcome her challenges and bloom even in difficult circumstances.
