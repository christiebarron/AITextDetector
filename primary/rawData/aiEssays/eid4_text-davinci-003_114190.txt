

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho symbolizes the protagonist's hope for the future. The end of winter symbolizes a new cycle of life and the protagonist's determined faith in eventual success. The words also express her determination and resilience to do better and to try again despite past failures - she will take the test again in the spring as the snows melt and the geese return. This message is hopeful and encourages readers to face their challenges with optimism.
