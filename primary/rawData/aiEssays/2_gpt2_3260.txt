"We are working in a very conservative way in trying to ensure this. When we have such big libraries that can withstand these kinds of pressures, we can say that there is something we need to do when people don't have access to literature and that is censorship."

Wenger has expressed support for open source research at different levels in her career, and on several occasion has called on the government to put a stop to such research. "I always thought some areas would have to be taken up by libraries. I consider them important people in the field of knowledge and research journalism," she said.

But she admitted she often felt that government would do better to promote open access to books.
