By using imagery of the spring and the geese that always return, the author is able to create a longing for things to return to the way they were before. By referencing the test at the end, the narrator is expressing her hope that she will be able to retake the test and pass it, so she can make something of her life. The narrator is looking forward to a brighter future and this is reflected in her use of hopeful language.
