

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho reflects the idea that each season presents a new beginning and each opportunity to make a new start. The protagonist is looking to the new season and the renewed cycle of life to turn a negative situation into a positive one. The winter hibiscus symbolizes resilience and hope in the face of adversity, and the protagonist looks to the coming of spring and the blooming of the hibiscus as a reminder to take the test again. This serves to emphasize the importance of perseverance and the power of renewal in order to succeed.
