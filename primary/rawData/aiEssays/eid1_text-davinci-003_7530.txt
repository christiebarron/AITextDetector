

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this quote because she wants to emphasize the idea of hope and perseverance. Winter Hibiscus is a story about a young Thai girl, Sisay, and her struggles to fit in as an immigrant in the United States. The story culminates with Sisay reflecting on her struggles, and how she can strive to succeed despite feeling like an outsider. The final line of the story symbolizes Sisay's hope for a better future - that she will continue to find her place and be able to thrive in the United States despite her circumstances. It reflects the idea that even in the darkest of times, there is always something to look forward to, and the possibility of success.
