

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho reflects the narrator's ambition and hope. At first, the narrator was dealing with the harsh realities of winter; however, knowing that spring will come compels the narrator to find resolution and positivity. The narrator looks forward to the arrival of spring with the promise of new beginnings, including the chance to take the test again. Spring symbolizes a time of renewal and growth, and the narrator is determined to make the most of the new season. The ending inspires readers to remain hopeful and to recognize the promise of new beginnings.
