The line reflects Mei Hua's determination to continue fighting and persisting no matter what difficulty she must face. She will prepare herself for the test she failed and tackle it again when life blooms in the spring. The imagery of the natural world in the line symbolizes hope and renewal, and Mei Hua's growth and strength of character through her journey from despair to perseverance.
