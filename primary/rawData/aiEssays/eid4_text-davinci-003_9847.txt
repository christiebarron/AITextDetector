

This ending serves as a reminder to the reader that nothing in life stays the same. It reinforces the idea that although the winter hibiscus blossoms have withered away, new life will come. The ending serves as a metaphor for the protagonist's struggles with school, and how even if they're struggling or feeling down one moment, things can always get better. Additionally, it gives the protagonist a sense of hope, that they can start again, that they can take the test again next year and that they are resilient.
