The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' has gone from gloomy and bleak to exhilarating. The music was strong, intense and emotional. There was something like two minutes of real pleasure. I never thought they would make it and then I would hear all three songs on a cassette. I can only imagine what life might have been like in that moment. It's been a huge blessing to see them making it again."

'From Home is' will be available from the publisher at select dates later this year, and on CD and233. This has had another impact on his current endeavours, since it has taken him ten weeks to complete the book.

The first of his novelisations of 'From Home is' will release from the UK on 7 February, with a full UK release expected later this year. He will also be publishing The End of a Tour album at that time.
