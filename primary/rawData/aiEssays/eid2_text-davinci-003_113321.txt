Materials that contain hate speech or are deemed to be inappropriate should not be available for public consumption in public libraries. A library is supposed to provide a place where people can go to learn, explore, and find reliable information. Censoring certain materials that are not suitable for public consumption can help ensure that the library remains a safe and reliable place for learning.
