

The ending is meant to suggest hope and determination. The speaker is anticipating a time of renewal and new beginnings. Despite facing an upsetting or difficult situation, the speaker is looking ahead and choosing to take a proactive outlook in order to make a positive change. Symbolically, the hibiscus speaks to the speaker of resilience despite the cold weather - it will bloom even in the hardest of times. In the same way, the speaker will take the test again knowing that it will bring new opportunities and possibilities in the spring.
