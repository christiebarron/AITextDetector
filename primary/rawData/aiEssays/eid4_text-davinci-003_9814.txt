

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho gives the protagonist hope. The protagonist has failed a test and feels disappointed. However, the image of the return of the geese and the budding of the hibiscus in spring tells the protagonist that not all will remain stagnant and that there will be another chance to try and succeed again. The ending leaves the protagonist with the idea that beauty and new beginnings will always exist. It shows that even in the face of failure and difficulty, hope and optimism always remain a possibility.
