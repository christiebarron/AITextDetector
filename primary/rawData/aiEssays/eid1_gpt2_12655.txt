Computers have many effects on people.

1. They reduce mental fatigue, increased social interaction, social participation and increased risk of obesity, diabetes, cardiovascular diseases and cancer.

2. As the U.S. government has increased the federal minimum wage from $7.25 an hour to $10.10 an hour, many economists think the changes will happen soon.

3. Their effects on health, happiness and quality of life will depend on the effect those two factors have on us.

The results of our study should not be conflated with the results of studies conducted by organizations of all stripes, or with studies that have examined our health, happiness and health effects.
