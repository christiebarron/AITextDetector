

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this powerful line to emphasize the power of perseverance and hope. The blossoming of the hibiscus represents the hope that spring brings and the narrator has to keep this hope in mind while studying for her test. This line shows that even though the narrator may have a difficult journey ahead of her, she will keep going because spring and growth are inevitable. This line also suggests that no matter what happens, there will always be hope and resilience at the heart of the story.
