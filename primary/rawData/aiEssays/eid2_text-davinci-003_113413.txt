It is important to consider whether certain materials should be removed from the library shelves if they are found to be offensive.

The library is the place we turn to when we want to explore a wide variety of topics, materials, and opinions. It should provide open access to a variety of material in a censorship-free environment.

On one hand, materials such as books, music, movies, and magazines should be available to patrons so that they can learn, develop their skills, and grow.
