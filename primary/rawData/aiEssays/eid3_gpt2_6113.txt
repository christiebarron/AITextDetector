Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways.


Rough Road Ahead - is rated on the following: High, Low, Difficult with high winds and thunderstorms and low or moderate winds that will not provide long enough lasting performance to be used in cycling.

Rough Road Ahead - features a range of settings - including braking, ground clearance, hill jumping, fast running speed - and it also includes 'Easy' (Low Tension) brake/steer on and off the tyre.
