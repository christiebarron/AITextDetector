

The ending reflects the theme of perseverance in the face of difficult odds and the idea of resilience, which is embodied by the winter hibiscus. The phrase, "When the snows melt and the geese return..." indicates the passage of time, which is necessary in order for the winter hibiscus to bloom. The protagonist knows that despite the difficulties she has faced, she will take the test again and move forward in life. The ending is hopeful and reinforces the idea of looking to the future with optimism.
