

The speaker of Winter Hibiscus, a twelve-year-old girl, is reflecting on her anxieties just before taking a crucial math test. She finds solace in the winter hibiscus that her aunt gave her, and the thought of how they will both return in the spring – the hibiscus blossoming anew and the girl taking the test again – reassures her. As such, the ending of the story symbolizes the hope and courage of perseverance in the face of difficulty.
