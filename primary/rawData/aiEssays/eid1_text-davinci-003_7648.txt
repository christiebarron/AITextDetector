

The ending line reflects the possibility that the protagonist will have another chance to take the test with more confidence. The winter hibiscus appears at the beginning of the story and symbolizes resilience of hope and optimism. The speaker has been struggling with a difficult test and it is this flower that gives them strength to continue. The returning of the spring, snows melting, and the geese symbolize renewal and growth, suggesting that there will be a time in the future when the protagonist can retake the test with a better frame of mind. The line provides readers with a feeling of optimism and hope that with the right mindset, the protagonist can overcome the difficulty of the test.
