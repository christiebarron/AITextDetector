

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho ties in to the theme of resilience. The protagonist and his grandfather have faced numerous obstacles throughout the book, and the ending serves to show the optimism and hope that they have for the future. The protagonist is determined to take the English test again when spring returns, showing his commitment and drive to overcome whatever hardships he may face. The imagery surrounding the hibiscus and the geese also plays into this theme as it emphasizes the idea of rebirth and returning to a better place.
