Too much time behind the computer screen can lead to less time for physical activities, social interaction and even educational activities. This can result in physical inactivity, social isolation and a decreased capacity for learning. In fact, studies have shown that too much computer usage can even impact people’s mental health in a negative way. 

Additionally, computers can have a negative effect on the environment. Computing devices require energy to run, and their constant usage consumes a lot of energy. Moreover, harmful emissions can be created through the manufacturing process of many computer components. And because computers contain many potentially toxic compounds, their disposal can also have a detrimental impact on the environment.
