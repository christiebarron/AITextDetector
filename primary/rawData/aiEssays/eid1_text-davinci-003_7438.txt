The melancholic mood of the poem is contrasted by the idea that each year brings new beginnings even in the face of seemingly insurmountable odds. The protagonist needs to believe in the possibility of success, and the hope that a better future will come in spite of any struggles. In this way, the poem serves as a reminder of the power of resilience and perseverance.
