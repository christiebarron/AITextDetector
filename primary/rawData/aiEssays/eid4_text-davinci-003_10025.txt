

The author uses the imagery of the Winter Hibiscus to depict the protagonist's difficult struggle in life. In the story, the protagonist takes an aptitude test multiple times, struggling to obtain a better result. At the end of the story, the protagonist is aware of the possible failure in taking the test again in spring; however, they take inspiration from the flower's resilience and determination to survive each winter, pushing them to hope for a more positive future. The winter hibiscus symbolizes their determination to push through the struggle and take the test again in the hope of a better result, in spite of the risks of failure.
