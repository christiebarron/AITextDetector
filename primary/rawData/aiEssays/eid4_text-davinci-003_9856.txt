

This quote is a reference to the main character's journey and their struggles that they have faced to this point in the story. The winter hibiscus, which serves as a symbol of hope and resilience, is indicative of the main character's journey. The quote therefore serves to show that despite the difficulties and challenges, and even if the main character does not pass the test this time, they will still continue to have hope in the future and eventually pass the test when the time comes. It is also symbolic of the idea that no matter how hard the journey may be, there will be moments of success and hope if one perseveres.
