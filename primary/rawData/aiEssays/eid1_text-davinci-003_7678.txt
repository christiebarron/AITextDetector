

The story ends this way because it is an allegory that speaks to overcoming adversity through resilience. The author is symbolic of the winter hibiscus that has to brave the elements and still bloom in the spring. The protagonist is the character who will take the test again after coming back in the spring as a rallying cry of hope and resiliency despite any struggles faced. The ending both wraps up the plot of the story and serves as a metaphor for how to overcome difficult times.
