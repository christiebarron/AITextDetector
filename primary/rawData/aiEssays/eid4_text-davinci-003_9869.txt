

The author wants to evoke a sense of hope in her readers that things will eventually improve. The imagery of melting snow and geese returning and a hibiscus budding is meant to paint a picture of renewal and growth. The line about taking the test again indicates that the protagonist is hopeful that they will be able to put the difficult aspects of their life behind them and start anew when the season changes. In this way, the hopeful imagery and the observation of the protagonist to take the test again highlights a sentiment of resilience and perseverance.
