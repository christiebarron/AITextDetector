

The narrator of the poem is reflecting on the life lessons she has learned from watching the hibiscus bloom every year. She has experienced that change is inevitable and acceptance is key in order for life to remain in balance. The statement at the end of the poem reflects the narrator's understanding that she will have the opportunity to take the test again in the future when the circumstances are different. The metaphor of the hibiscus reflects these themes and by ending with this statement, the narrator is expressing her confidence in her ability to accept change and take life's challenges head on.
