

This ending serves as a symbol of hope and renewal. The hibiscus, which was alive and vibrant in the beginning of the story, is a representation of the protagonist's resilience. By ending the story with the protagonist's promise to retake the test in spring when the hibiscus has blossomed, Ho is conveying two messages. First, it conveys hope that the protagonist's circumstances will change for the better. Second, it conveys a message of perseverance and resilience in the face of adversity. No matter how challenging the circumstances, the protagonist seeks renewal and doggedly pursues her goals. This resonates with readers and highlights themes of fresh starts, resilience, and hope.
