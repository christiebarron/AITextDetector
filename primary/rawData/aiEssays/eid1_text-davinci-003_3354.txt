

Dear Editor, 

I am writing to share my opinion on the effects computers have on people and to persuade the readers to agree with me.

In today's tech-driven world, computers are commonplace. They are necessary tools for communication and productivity. Computers can help us work faster and more efficiently, allowing us to accomplish more than ever before. They make it easier to organize, store, and share data. Computers also offer a way for people to connect with one another around the world.

However, it is important to recognize the potential negative effects of computers on people. People can become too reliant on computers, leading to decreased creativity, problem solving skills, and communication skills. Additionally, spending too much time on the computer can lead to physical health problems, such as vision or neck problems. 

Despite these potential pitfalls, I still believe that computers offer far more positives than negatives. When used responsibly, computers can be a valuable tool for connecting, researching, and producing work. They enable us to have instant access to a wealth of knowledge and to stay connected with the world, even from the comfort of our own homes.
