

The ending of the poem alludes to the perseverance of the speaker despite their difficult situation. Despite failing the test that is the main focus of the poem, the speaker looks forward to another opportunity to succeed in the future. The poem speaks to the importance of resilience in the face of challenges and hardships. The imagery of the blossoming hibiscus reminds the reader of the changing of the seasons and in turn, the changing of fortune. This final line provides a sense of hope and optimism that the speaker will overcome their recent failure.
