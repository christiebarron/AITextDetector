

The story Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is about a young girl, Jenna, who grows a hibiscus plant in her bedroom as something to care for and nurture during the cold winter months. The ending of the story symbolizes Jenna's determination and hope that she will find success despite the winter that is challenging her. She is determined to try again and succeed after she grows her plant and witnesses its beauty and eventual blooming. Through the hibiscus, she finds the strength to continue forward no matter how difficult her current situation may be. By ending with the promise of trying again after the plant blooms, Jenna continues to demonstrate her resilience and hopes for a better future.
