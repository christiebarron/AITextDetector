

At the beginning of the story, the speaker, Ahmong, is about to take a test that she is uncertain she can pass. The instructor tells Ahmong that if she passes the test, she'll have the opportunity to intern at the local hospital. By ending with this statement, the author signifies that Ahmong's future hangs in the balance. The hopeful imagery of springtime symbolizes Ahmong's potential for a brighter future, suggesting that if she takes the test again in the spring, she'll have a chance to achieve her dreams. This leaves readers with a hopeful message that if Ahmong returns in the spring ready to take the test again, she might be able to make her dreams a reality.
