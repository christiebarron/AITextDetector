

Minfong Ho ends the story Winter Hibiscus with this line to show the narrator's faith in her hard work and perseverance. The imagery of the snows melting and the geese returning is meant to symbolize the idea that no matter how difficult things may be during the winter, one can make it through a difficult time with the idea of spring slowly coming. The narrator finds a sense of hope in the possibility of things returning better than before and taking a test again is symbolic of her determination to make something out of her hard work despite her fears.
