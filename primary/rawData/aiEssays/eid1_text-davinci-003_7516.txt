

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is symbolic of hope and perseverance. The speaker is recalling a moment of failure in their life, when they failed a test. The speaker expresses their hope that come springtime, when the winter and its cold weather has passed and the hibiscus that the speaker has planted has begun to bloom, that they will have another chance to try again and pass the test. The hope expressed in the ending emphasizes the idea that no one should give up even if they are faced with failure or disappointment; instead, they should take what they have learned and try again.
