

The narrator of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho resolves to take the test again when the spring arrives. This serves as closure to the narrative and encapsulates the narrator's determination to solve the problem. It also symbolizes hope and optimism, and illustrates that no matter how challenging a situation may be, one can always gain strength through perseverance and resilience. By reflecting on the inevitable return of spring, the narrator has been able to find peace and even positivity, in the present moment, despite facing difficulty.
