

The ending of this poem conveys the deep desire of the child to challenge herself, despite the hardships of the winter. The hibiscus symbolizes growth, progress, and hope, and the hope of the child that if she patiently and diligently perseveres, she will be able to take the test again. By using these symbols, this poem conveys a lesson of perseverance, hope, and determination in the face of difficulty. By ending with a clear vision of the child taking the test again, it also offers a sense of comfort and encouragement to the reader. Despite the hardships of the winter, the child is confident and determined that she will come out successful.
