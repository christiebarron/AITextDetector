

The ending of the poem is meant to serve as a hopeful reminder of the narrator's inner strength and resilience. The imagery of the hibiscus blooming, the geese returning, and the snow melting is used to symbolize rebirth and renewal. By ending with the narrator's hope of taking the test again in the future, the poem conveys the narrator's determination and faith in a brighter future. It is a poignant reminder that even in difficult times, hope and strength can still be found.
