

The narrator of this poem, a student, is having difficulty studying for a test. The student finds peace in looking at the winter hibiscus flowers in his aunt's backyard, making him feel connected to his home and culture. By ending the poem with the student's statement, Ho is emphasizing the importance of learning and understanding one's home and culture in order to succeed in life. With the reminder that the student will take the test again in the spring, the poem communicates an overall message of hope and inspiration.
