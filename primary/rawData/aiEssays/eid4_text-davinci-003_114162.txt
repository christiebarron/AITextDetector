

The author is using the metaphor of winter hibernusc to illustrate her own experience of failing a test. By ending the poem with a hopeful tone, the author implies that just as the winter hibiscus will return in the spring, she is also determined to retake the test and persevere. This idea of resilience and hope in the face of adversity is the main takeaway of the poem and the author expresses this sentiment by ending with this hopeful line.
