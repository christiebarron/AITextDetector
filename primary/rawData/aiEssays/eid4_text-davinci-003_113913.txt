

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this line to symbolize the idea of hope in the face of adversity. Winter is a difficult time, symbolized by the student's struggles in school. Nevertheless, despite this feeling of stagnation, Ho suggests that spring will eventually come. This brings with it new opportunities and a chance for renewal, symbolized by the blooming hibiscus and the student's decision to take the test again. The line leaves the reader with an uplifting feeling and encourages them to think about the possibility of persevering even in the difficult moments of life.
