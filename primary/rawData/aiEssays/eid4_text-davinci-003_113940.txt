

The author ends Winter Hibiscus with this line because it symbolizes hope and a fresh start. The hibiscus symbolizes the protagonist's future - that with hard work and perseverance, they can achieve their goals. Just as the hibiscus blooms in the spring, so too will the protagonist's success come when they retake the test. The line conveys the message that they won't give up, that they will keep trying until they reach their goal.
