

Minfong Ho's Winter Hibiscus ends with this hope-filled expression to convey a message of perseverance and resilience. The protagonist views her upcoming test as an opportunity to demonstrate how far she has come since the start of winter. The winter and the snows melting symbolize the end of a tough period, and the return of the geese suggests a fresh start and the promise of a new beginning. The hibiscus plant budding signifies the protagonist's potential to discover her new self and chase her dreams, reinforcing her commitment to make the most of the second chance afforded to her. The conclusion gives us a glimpse of how the protagonist is looking forward to this new era with determination, eagerness, and optimism, and conveys the idea that things may become difficult but never impossible, because with every hurdle comes a new opportunity for growth.
