I am writing to express my opinion on the effects computers have on people. For many years, computers have become an integral part of our lives, making it easier to stay connected and get work done quickly. While there are benefits to having access to these powerful machines, there are also some potential drawbacks.

As computers become more advanced and omnipresent, people spend more of their time on them than ever before. This can lead to an increased reliance on technology and an overall decrease in physical activity and outdoor time. Furthermore, with the rise of social media, people are more likely to spend excessive amounts of time comparing themselves to others online, causing a decrease in self-confidence.

In addition, there is a risk that computer use can lead to a reduced attention span and a decrease in the ability to think critically and creatively. This can derail productivity and hinder our ability to interact in real-life social situations.
