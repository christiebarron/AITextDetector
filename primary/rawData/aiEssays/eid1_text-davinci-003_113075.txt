I am writing to express my opinion on the effects computers have had on people's lives. Over the past few decades, computers have transformed the way we live, work, and interact with one another. With their powerful computing capabilities and their widespread use, it's hard to deny that computers have had a significant impact on our lives.

While computers have certainly had a positive effect, allowing us to do more in less time and providing us with access to an unparalleled array of information, they have also had a negative effect. Many people now find themselves spending an excessive amount of time in front of a computer screen, which can lead to physical and mental health problems such as migraines, vision problems, obesity, and even depression. 

On top of this, we have become increasingly dependent on computers to handle daily tasks, to the point where our reliance on technology has begun to erode our basic skills. We can no longer rely on our own memory or problem-solving skills; instead, we turn to computers for everything from grocery lists to even simple math calculations. Our attention spans are quickly diminishing as well; the ability to pay attention and focus on a single task or to have meaningful conversations has become diminished.
