

The winter hibiscus in the story is a symbol of hope, aspiration, and determination. The protagonist of the story is a student who is struggling to learn English and preparing to take an English language test. The student is anxious and worries about failing the test. By ending the story with the anticipation of the student's rebirth in the spring when the hibiscus is budding, Minfong Ho is reinforcing the idea that despite the difficulty of learning English, the student has the determination and resilience to try again and will eventually succeed in the test. The winter hibiscus symbolizes the idea that the student will persevere in their studies and emerge stronger and more hopeful.
