

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho signifies hope and resilience. It reflects the narrator's determination to have a better outcome in the future. By ending with the narrator's promise to retake the test when the winter is gone, symbolizes their dedication to continue to strive for success despite the difficulties they are going through and the obstacles that they have already faced.  The hopeful ending is an empowering moment that conveys the themes of hard work, persistence, and resiliency to never give up.
