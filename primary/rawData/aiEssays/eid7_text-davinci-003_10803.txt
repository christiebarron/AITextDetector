

I was looking for a new job and had been on countless interviews, but I was not successful in getting the job. I began to feel frustrated and discouraged, especially as I continued to be rejected.

Then one day, I got a call about a job that I had applied for weeks before. I was so excited to be able to go in for a second round of interviews. During the interview, I was asked tough questions that I could tell the hiring managers already knew the right answer to. Despite my lack of knowledge, I didn't panic. I remained calm and patient, was honest and open with my responses, and took the time to think about each answer before speaking.

My patience paid off - I was offered the job on the spot and have been happily employed in the role for three years now. It is all thanks to my determination, perseverance, and patience to remain positive in a difficult situation.
