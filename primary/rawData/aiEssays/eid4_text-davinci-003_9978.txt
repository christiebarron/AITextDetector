

At the end of the story, the protagonist's teacher tells her to come back and take the math test again in the spring so she can be promoted from 8th grade to 9th. The protagonist is determined to pass the test and stay with her friends to attend high school together. The ending of the story reflects her hope for the future and her determination to improve her math skills and pass the test. By ending the story with the protagonist's thoughts of returning in the spring to take the test, the author indicates that the protagonist will later achieve her goal when the conditions of the natural world around her, symbolized by the hibiscus, changes with the seasons.
