

The narrator of the story is talking about how she had to take a Mandarin test for school and failed it. She was embarrassed and ashamed, and she had just found a Winter Hibiscus in her backyard. This Winter Hibiscus had managed to bloom in the cold winter weather, so she took courage from it. She then decides to take the Mandarin test again when spring comes and the Winter Hibiscus is in full bloom. This ending is meaningful because it expresses the narrator's hope and perseverance. She takes strength from the Winter Hibiscus and is determined to overcome her struggles and succeed in her studies.
