Our world is becoming more and more reliant on technology, and computers have become a central part of our lives throughout the past 30 years or so. As someone who has been at the forefront of the technology age, I am keenly aware of the many incredible benefits that computers offer.

However, I am also concerned about the drawbacks that come with having this technology at our fingertips. Computers have created a divide between the physical world and the digital world. While it can be incredibly empowering to stay connected, relying on computers too much can lead to an unhealthy disconnect from our own bodies and the environment.

Rather than spending our days in front of a computer, it's important for each individual to find a healthy balance between digital and physical activities. Going out for a walk or taking a yoga class can help us reestablish our connection to the physical world and recharge our mind and body.

The changes that come with this technology age are inevitable, but they don't have to be negative. Staying conscious about how we use computers and actively engaging in physical activities can be beneficial for our physical and mental health.
