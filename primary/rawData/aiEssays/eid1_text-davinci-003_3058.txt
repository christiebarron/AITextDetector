

Dear Editor,

I am writing to express my opinion on the effects computers have on people, and to persuade your readers to agree with me.

Computers can bring the world to a person's fingertips. People can access information from anywhere on the planet and engage with people from other countries with ease. Computers can also enable people to stay connected with their friends and family members, regardless of how far away they may live. Computers also open the door for people to broaden their knowledge and pursue hobbies and interests that were previously inaccessible to them.

However, computers can also have a negative impact on people. Unregulated or inappropriate use of computers can lead to an addiction that opens the door for cyberbullying, unwanted contact, and other threatening behaviour. Overuse can also lead to physical health issues such as repetitive strain injury and computer vision syndrome.

Therefore, I believe that it is essential for people of all ages to learn how to use computers safely and appropriately. To ensure people use computers properly, they should be taught the importance of implementing safety protocols, such as using passwords, restricting access, and setting time limits.
