

Dear Editor, 

I am writing to express my opinion about computers and the impact they have on us. First, I firmly believe that computers have had a tremendous influence over people’s lives, both positively and negatively. 

On the plus side, having access to the internet through computers means we are able to be better informed and stay up to date with global events. Furthermore, with the aid of the numerous programs available to us, carrying out mundane tasks can be achieved in a fraction of the time. Not to mention, with the invention of smartphones, they now fit in our pocket and allow us to stay connected no matter where we are. 

However, on the downside, computers can lead to a more sedentary lifestyle. For some, it can be difficult to tear away from their computer when the time calls for it. In addition, computers can be a breeding ground for too much idle time, which if it isn’t managed wisely, can lead to cyber addiction.
