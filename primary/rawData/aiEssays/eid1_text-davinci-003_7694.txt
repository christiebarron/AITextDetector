

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho reflects the protagonist's determination to overcome their struggles. By returning to the idea of the hibiscus in the spring, the protagonist is expressing hope that their life will improve with the end of winter and the start of a new season. This motif of renewal symbolizes the protagonist's persistence in trying again, no matter the outcome of the test. The protagonist will always have the opportunity to try again, just like the hibiscus blooms again in the spring.
