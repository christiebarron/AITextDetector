

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho reflects how the protagonist is looking to the future, finding hope and comfort in the promise of what is to come. The protagonist is taking an exam, but they feel overwhelmed and discouraged with their lack of knowledge. By ending with an image of a hibiscus blooming in the spring, the protagonist is reframing the situation with optimism. The returning of the geese and the melting of the snow signify renewal and the promise of a brighter future. The protagonist is reassured that with time they will regain enough confidence and knowledge to face the exam again.
