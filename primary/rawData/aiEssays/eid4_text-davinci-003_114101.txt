

The ending speaks to gathering strength from adversity. Winter Hibiscus is a story about the main character, a teenage girl, Mei Li, learning how to make her own opportunities. She entered a poetry contest, but failed to win the prize she was hoping for. 

At the end of the story, Mei Li finds solace in the fact that the winter hibiscus, a reminder of the perseverance of her family, will bloom again in the spring. The quote reminds us that, like the winter hibiscus, Mei Li still has an opportunity to take the poetry test again in the spring. The quote is a symbol of hope and resilience for Mei Li. It suggests that never giving up is important in order to reach success in life.
