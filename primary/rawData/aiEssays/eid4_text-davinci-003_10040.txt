

The protagonist in the story is a twelve-year-old girl who is struggling in school due to her language barrier. At the end of the story, she decides to try again when the hibiscus returns the following spring. This symbolizes her hope for a new beginning and the strength to persevere despite her challenges. The flowers are a metaphor for her own growth and development. By using the hibiscus as a symbol, Minfong Ho is showing the reader that the protagonist will be able to overcome her struggles and succeed in life if she is able to take ownership of her learning and makes the effort to improve.
