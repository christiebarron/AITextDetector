Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways. In fact, many consider it one of the best bike paths in the city (although some have tried to restrict cyclists from using the road, and some have asked others not to use it). At the same time, it can be quite dangerous.

Rough roads are often marked in order to avoid causing further traffic problems, but cyclists have reported being hit by cars and parked on street corners, and having to use the cycle-track.

If you are aware of other problems running and riding the road glorious roads, do make sure that you have a bicycle safety certificate and it is currently available for inspection.

If there are any additional cycling safety problems you should know of, please post in our forum section below or contact us at roading-safety@theadidas.com for more information.
