

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with the hope that the protagonist, Li Li, will be able to take her exam again in the spring when the snows thaw and the geese return. Li Li did not pass her exams when she was first tested, and her studies have been interrupted due to the weather conditions. The imagery of the hibiscus plant, which is a beautiful flower that blooms even in the winter, serves as a symbol of hope that she will succeed in her exams if given another chance. The ending represents Li Li's determination to better herself by passing the exam when spring arrives.
