

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves to emphasize the resilience of the protagonist and her determination to succeed. The protagonist has faced a series of challenges, including having to move away from her home, face cultural differences, and take a difficult test. Despite all these challenges, the protagonist is determined to take the test again when she returns home in the spring and the flowers are in bloom. The symbolism of the hibiscus budding in the spring serves as a reminder of the protagonist's perseverance and determination in the face of adversity.
