

The ending of this poem is hopeful and reflective. The speaker has been contemplating the idea of taking a test. The imagery of the winter hibiscus is used to symbolize the idea of hope - its beauty remains even when it's dormant in winter. The speaker makes the connection between this hope and taking the test, asking that when the hibiscus is blooming again in spring, they will have the courage and the hope to attempt the test again. By ending the poem with hope for the future, the speaker suggests the idea of having the courage to continue to try even in the face of challenges and failure.
