

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho with 'When they come back in the spring, when the snows melt and the geese return and this hibiscus is budding, then I will take that test again' is symbolic of hope and perseverance. Even when faced with difficult circumstances, like missing a test due to the snow, there is still a chance to come back stronger and do better. The phrase also suggests that the character will have inherited wisdom from the difficult experience and will be better prepared to face the test in the spring. This ending reflects the themes of resilience and perseverance in the story, sending a hopeful message to the reader.
