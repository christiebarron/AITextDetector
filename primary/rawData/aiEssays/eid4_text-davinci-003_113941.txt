

The book Winter Hibiscus ends with this question because it symbolizes hope and perseverance. The winter hibiscus is seen as a symbol of hope, as it continues to grow even during the cold winter. Additionally, the protagonist, Yune, is about to take a test that could determine her future. However, she is uncertain about the results. With the question at the end, it emphasizes Yune's determination to keep striving and never give up, no matter what the outcome of her test. The phrase "when they come back in the spring" symbolizes a rebirth and a new beginning, and reflects Yune's desire to continue to make a better future for herself.
