

Minfong Ho ends the story with this line to signify the hope that Winter Hibiscus (the protagonist) has for the future. The return of the geese and the budding of the hibiscus symbolize new growth and the possibility of success after overcoming hardships. By ending the story with these hopeful symbols, Ho implies that, despite the difficulties and unfairness Winter faced, she still has faith that she can persevere and achieve her goals.
