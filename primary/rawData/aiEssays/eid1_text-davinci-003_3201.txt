I am writing to express my opinion on the effects computers have on people. As someone who has seen their impact firsthand, I can attest to the fact that they are immensely beneficial.

The most apparent effect of computers on people is their convenience. No longer are individuals required to search for relevant information by physically poring over books. Rather, the same information can be easily accessed online within minutes. People now have access to a vast array of resources and can take advantage of them for all sorts of activities such as researching, communication and entertainment.

Computers also improve social connections, as people can easily connect with each other regardless of where they are. With the development of instant messaging software and social media platforms, people of all ages have the opportunity to stay in touch with their loved ones. This ability to stay connected has become invaluable in our increasingly global society.

Finally, computers open up numerous opportunities for individuals to pursue their passions. Reading blogs, discovering new music, and creating content are only some of the things people can  do with the help of computers.
