

The ending of "Winter Hibiscus" offers a sense of hope and optimism to the protagonist about their future. Through this ending, the author emphasizes the idea that the winter season may be difficult, but that eventually, there will be a new season with new opportunities. The protagonist is determined to take back control of their life by taking the test again when the hibiscus blooms, a symbol of growth and renewal. This reflects the theme of resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.
