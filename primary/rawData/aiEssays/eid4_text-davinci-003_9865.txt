

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is meant to suggest hope and resiliency. The protagonist has faced many challenges and has been feeling discouraged. The ending shows that despite the setback, they are determined to persevere and try again. The imagery of spring, melting snow, and returning geese represent the cycle of nature and its eventual renewal, signaling a new opportunity and a chance for the protagonist to strive to reach their goal.
