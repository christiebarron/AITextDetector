

The ending of Winter Hibiscus reflects the beauty of hope and renewal. The protagonist of the story is a young girl living in a small Chinese village who dreams of studying at a university in Shanghai. While studying for her entrance exam, her own hibiscus plant blooms in the midst of the winter snow, giving her the strength and confidence to continue pursuing her dreams. 

The ending line of the story speaks to the idea that the protagonist will always find solace in the natural beauty and peace of her home, even if she fails or the path she chooses doesn't pan out. In the moment of seeing her hibiscus bloom in the snow, the narrator has renewed hope and faith that she can come back and try again in the spring - a time of beauty and growth. This ultimate message of hope and renewal is a fitting ending to the story.
