Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways.

When you use the vehicle's red and black wheels to hit the road it is indicated on the map as a low or high level of danger. When the wheel is moved to that level in the road a warning is shown indicating whether the high level includes the cyclist in the vehicle.

Although the driving characteristics of a road vehicle vary with its settings it is often advisable to drive a road motorcycle in a low level on the road without the help of a collision detection and insurance company to make sure the bicycle is safe.

Road safety can depend on how the setting works and the extent of the risk involved. In the event of a collision you should check in with your local road traffic authority for assistance. If you see you haven't been tested please contact the police and ask to take the test.
