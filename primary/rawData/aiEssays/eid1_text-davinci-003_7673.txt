

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho echoes the themes of hope and resolution for the protagonist. The ending symbolizes the protagonist's determination to not give up and take control of her life despite the challenges she faces. The hibiscus is a symbol of resilience, growth, and hope, and through this metaphor the protagonist is ultimately encouraged to keep actively striving to achieve her goals despite any obstacles she may face. The protagonist hopes to take the tests again in the spring when the hibiscus buds, indicating that she has renewed hope and resolve to continue moving forward and persevere.
