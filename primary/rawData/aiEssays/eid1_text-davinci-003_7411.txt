

The ending of Winter Hibiscus reflects a theme of hope and perseverance throughout the story. The protagonist resolves to retake the test, symbolizing her progress and dedication. She will take the test when conditions are better, when the snows melt and birds return. This is a sign of rebirth and of a potential better future. It gives hope to readers that despite the challenges faced in life, there is always an opportunity for improvement and the ability for the future to be even brighter than the present.
