

I remember when I was in high school, I had taken on a large class load, and even though I was attempting my best, I was having a tough time juggling it all. As the semester was nearing an end, I was really struggling and had very little time left to turn everything in before the deadline.

When I went to my teacher to ask for some extra time, I was expecting to be turned away and get lectured on doing better planning in the future. To my surprise, my teacher was extremely patient with my situation. He listened to my explanation, patiently answered my questions, and ultimately gave me an extension. I was incredibly relieved and grateful for the extra time.

It was heartening to know that my teacher was truly invested in my academic success and willing to be patient in order to make it possible. That experience really taught me the importance of patience and understanding in difficult times.
