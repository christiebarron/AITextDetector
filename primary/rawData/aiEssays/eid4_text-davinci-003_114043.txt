

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with 'When they come back in the spring, when the snows melt and the geese return and this hibiscus is budding, then I will take that test again?' to emphasize the power of nature, and the passing of time, as the cycle of life continues. The line hints at the idea that time is a great healer, and the passing of the winter and the coming of the spring will bring a sense of optimism and hope. The speaker of the story is facing a difficult exam, and is looking to the promise of the future and the cyclical nature of life for hope and reassurance.
