

The symbolism of the line helps to emphasize the narrator's growth. The plants, geese, and snow serve as reminders of nature's cyclical nature and the narrator's growing understanding of the cycle. It also conveys that although the winter season may be a challenge, there will be a time of renewal and opportunities to take tests again when the spring comes. This can also be seen as a metaphor for how, with patience and hope, any challenge can be ultimately overcome.
