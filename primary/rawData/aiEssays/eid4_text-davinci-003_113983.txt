

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is meant to symbolize hope. The protagonist is discussing how some flowers may cease to exist and how they must overcome odds in order to survive. The ending implies that the protagonist will face a similar struggle and must demonstrate resilience in order to succeed. By ending with the protagonist deciding to take the test again, it shows that they have a newfound determination and optimism to face whatever challenges come their way. It is meant to inspire readers to do the same.
