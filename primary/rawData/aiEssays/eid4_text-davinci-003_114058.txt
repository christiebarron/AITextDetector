

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is symbolic of renewal and hope. The protagonist is facing a difficult test that represents a major obstacle in her life. By ending with the image of the hibiscus budding in the spring, the author is suggesting that, although the protagonist currently feels overwhelmed and discouraged, her situation will improve eventually. The message of the ending is that although life can be difficult, with strength and perseverance, one can overcome their obstacles and find success.
