

The ending of this poem serves to emphasize the power of hope and perseverance in the face of adversity. The narrator has struggled to pass a test for many years, but despite her failures, she remains determined. She is suggesting that in due course, the "winter" in her life will pass and she will find success. The beauty of springtime, and the metaphor of the blooming hibiscus serve as physical representations of that hope and determination for her. The narrator is looking forward to a time when she will be able to put her troubles behind her, and finally realize her dreams.
