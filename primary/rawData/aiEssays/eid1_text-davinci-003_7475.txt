

At the end of the story, the protagonist, Lek, has just failed a geography test. This is a real setback for her, as she is already struggling since she and her family have recently moved to a new place and settled into precarious living conditions. Despite this, she is determined to be successful and pass the test when it comes around next. The author ends with this hopeful image of Lek persevering and triumphing, emphasizing her strength in the face of everyday challenges.
