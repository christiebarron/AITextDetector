

The author is using the hibiscus flower as a metaphor for hope. After months of winter and the bleakness that accompanies it, by the spring season the flower will have grown, just as the protagonist will have the opportunity to take the test again. The metaphor of the flower blooming also symbolizes the resilience of the protagonist, who hopefully will have gained the knowledge and confidence needed to take the test and pass it. The ending of the story is intended to be hopeful and encouraging for the protagonist.
