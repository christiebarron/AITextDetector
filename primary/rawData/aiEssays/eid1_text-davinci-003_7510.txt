

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho emphasizes the main character's resilience, following her struggle with her schoolwork. Her family's garden of winter hibiscus is a metaphor for the main character's determination to persevere despite difficult circumstances. By having the story end with the main character vowing to take the test again in the spring when the hibiscus is budding, Ho demonstrates her determination and commitment to overcome adversity. The image of the winter hibiscus at the end is a symbol of hope for better things to come.
