The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' is so high that when it finally arrives in cinemas in April, it is "probably among the most emotional films in cinema history".

And yet the director insists that it is just as much a love story, rather than it being about her husband's death as it is about her dad's.

"I don't always choose a bad ending," he says, describing the choice to start from something that feels "just right".
