

The narrator of the story is a young student who failed a test just as the winter approached. By ending the story with the hope of the spring and of a second chance, it can be seen as a representation of overcoming difficulty and of not giving up. The narrator finds strength in their belief that when the snows melt and they have the chance to take the test again, they will be prepared. This optimism and view of resilience is a reflection of the narrator's character and provides an uplifting sentiment to the story.
