

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho emphasizes perseverance in the face of a difficult challenge. The protagonist had failed a math test earlier in the story, and the ending serves as a reminder of her perseverance and determination. By linking the protagonist's eventual success to the arrival of spring, it emphasizes the natural cycle of the seasons and serves as a symbol of the main character's resilience and optimism. The ending also implies that success will come to those who never give up and who keep trying.
