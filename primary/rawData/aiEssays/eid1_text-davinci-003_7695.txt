

Minfong Ho uses the idea of a cycle of seasons to bring a sense of hope and possibility to the ending of her story. The idea of the hibiscus blooming in the spring suggests a rebirth, which speaks to the idea of starting anew. In ending her story with the protagonist preparing to take the test again, Minfong Ho suggests that a new journey is beginning, and that the protagonist is growing and learning from their experiences. It brings a sense of optimism to the story and demonstrates that the protagonist has perseverance and hope for the future.
