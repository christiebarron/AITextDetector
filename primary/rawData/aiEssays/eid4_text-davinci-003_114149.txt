

This ending emphasizes the cyclic nature of the natural world and the importance of understanding and respecting it. By emphasizing the changes that will happen upon the return of geese and the dawning of spring, the author encourages readers to appreciate changes in their own lives and the lives of those around them. This ending also reinforces the idea that no matter the challenges one might face, it is possible to persevere and come out stronger. The hibiscus, despite enduring harsh winter conditions, will come back in full bloom in the spring, showing that it is possible to withstand and overcome difficult situations, and become stronger as a result.
