

The narrator of this poem is a young student struggling to find motivation to finish their school assignments. The poem ends with the narrator waiting for spring as an inspiration to help finish their school work. The narrator is comparing the blooming of the winter hibiscus with their own growth, and the melting snow and the return of geese to their return to motivation. The narrator is suggesting that by waiting for the return of spring, their assignments will cease to be daunting and they will be able to take the test again with newfound confidence and knowledge.
