

Minfong Ho's ending emphasizes the resilience of the protagonist and her community. The protagonist is determined to take the test again, no matter what the outcome; the imagery of the snows melting, the geese returning, and the hibiscus budding symbolizes rebirth and renewal. This reflects her focus on learning and growing, despite the difficult circumstances she and her family have experienced. The ending also reflects the theme of hope and the idea that life can go on in difficult times. Even though the protagonist has experienced rejection and failure, she is determined to keep striving forward.
