The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' was a moment of sadness. "People in the countryside would wake up after the break and ask how I felt. They were all very lonely. I tried to write 'From Home is' as a gesture of support for everyone involved." But it is the book that shows how far away it is from reality and even the end of life.

The way of my narrator – 'Hairy and beautiful' – is also a reflection of how one feels about oneself, by being as close, so as not to let go of it.
