

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is meant to illustrate the idea of resilience and hope in the face of adversity. The protagonist is considering taking the same difficult test again after the winter season, which could symbolize the struggles of life and the endurance of those who persist in the face of hardship. In this way, the ending reflects the ultimate message of the story: despite setbacks, people can find hope and courage and strive to succeed.
