

The protagonist of the story, Seng, has to decide what to do about her education, which is put on hold as she and her family are refugees from their home country. The ending line is symbolic as she wonders if she will have the opportunity to retake the test in the spring when the geese return and the flowers begin to bloom, symbolizing the possibility of a new beginning and a hopeful future. The line also serves as a reminder that no matter what the future holds, she will have to make decisions that will shape her life.
