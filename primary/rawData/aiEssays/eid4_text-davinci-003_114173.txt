

The narrator of the poem is a young girl who is reflecting on a difficult math test that she has recently taken. By ending the poem with this sentiment, the author is emphasizing the idea of hope. The imagery of spring, with melting snow and geese returning, is symbolic of new growth and new beginnings. This reflects the girl's hope of eventually passing the test when she takes it again. This serves to portray the uplifting message of perseverance and resilience in the face of challenge and difficulty.
