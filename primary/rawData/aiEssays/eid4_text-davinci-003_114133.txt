

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho was chosen to emphasize the message of perseverance and hope that is present throughout the story. The protagonist chooses to take the test again when the hibiscus blooms in the spring, as a reminder that just like the flower, she can take the test again and will not be broken by her failures. The ending serves as a reminder that with the right attitude and determination, anything can be achieved. It also reflects the natural cycle of growth and rebirth that is shown throughout the story.
