

The author, Minfong Ho, effectively conveys a sense of hope and renewal for her protagonist. Throughout the story, the protagonist has faced several challenges that have tested her strength and resilience. By symbolically linking the protagonist’s success or failure with the fate of the Winter Hibiscus, Minfong Ho creates a powerful metaphor that provides a sense of closure for the story. The promise in the final line, “When they come back in the spring...then I will take that test again”, is a message of resilience and faith. It conveys to the reader a powerful message that however tough the present is, the future holds hope and opportunity for a better and brighter tomorrow.
