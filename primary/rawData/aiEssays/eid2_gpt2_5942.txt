As a result of Mr Nye's comments, the UK has withdrawn an "action" against copyright infringement.

In February, a British court ruled that a court had improperly used a ban on digital rights in the UK to ban an online petition.

The ruling, which will rule May has to appeal, came in response to an earlier ruling that a court should uphold a government order requiring a publisher to turn over copies of a British court order for the release of court filings in other countries.

A similar ruling was issued in October, which found the UK had not proved that it could comply with the law.
