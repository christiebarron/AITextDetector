

The ending is significant because it signifies the amount of effort the protagonist is putting in her education and her determination to improve her academic performance. The imagery of the winter hibiscus is used to symbolize resilience and hard work. The narrator is looking ahead and wanting to take her test again in the spring demonstrating her willingness to learn and grow. This idea is reinforced by the imagery of geese and melting snow which reflect the passage of time and the coming of a new season. With this hopeful and determined outlook, the protagonist is looking forward to a brighter future.
