The ending phrase is symbolic of looking forward to a new beginning, which is represented by the return of the geese, the blooming of the hibiscus, and her eventual taking of the English proficiency test. This phrase speaks to the protagonist’s strength and optimism in the face of adversity. By emphasizing the possibility of renewal and of success, it serves as a source of hope and inspiration to readers who can relate to the protagonist’s story.
