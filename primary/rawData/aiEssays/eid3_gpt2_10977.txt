Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways.

'Rough Road Ahead' enables the "roll back" of the path the cyclist is proceeding on in one direction and provides for the driver (not on-ramp) to overtake the cyclist, allowing the bicyclist to cross the path faster.

A driver's hand must be in the driver's wheel but on the path to the cyclists. This can cause problems, not just to the rider but also the driver.
