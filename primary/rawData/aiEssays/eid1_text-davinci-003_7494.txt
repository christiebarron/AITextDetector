

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this imagery to symbolize the main character's perseverance and resilience in the face of adversity. The hibiscus is a symbol of hope and determination. The character has failed an English test due to the language barrier but commits to taking it again in the future. The snows melting, geese returning, and hibiscus budding suggest that despite the harsh winter and the challenge of the language barrier, there is still hope for spring. By symbolizing hope through this imagery, Minfong Ho conveys the message that while the current situation may seem bleak and insurmountable, there is still potential for a brighter future.
