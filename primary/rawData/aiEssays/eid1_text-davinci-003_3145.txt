We can use our computers to research information, connect with people from around the world, and even work remotely. Computers have allowed us to stay connected with friends and family, even if they are located on different continents.

Additionally, people may find themselves using their computers to communicate instead of interacting with others in person. This can lead to feelings of isolation and loneliness.
