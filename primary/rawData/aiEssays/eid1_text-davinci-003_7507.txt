

Minfong Ho ends the story with the main character, Mei, knowing that the hibiscus will bloom again in the spring. This symbolic closure reminds the reader that there will always be times of growth, when things can turn for the better. When the hibiscus is in bloom, Mei will have the chance to retake her test and she will be ready for it. This is a hopeful message, that no matter how hard situations might seem, better times are always ahead.
