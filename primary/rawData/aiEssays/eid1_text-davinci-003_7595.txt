

At the end of the poem, Minfong Ho is reflecting on how the winter hibiscus is a metaphor for resilience and determination, and how both of these traits are beneficial in tackling life's struggles. The final line of the poem suggests that the speaker will take another shot at achieving her goals, no matter how difficult they may be, because she has the strength and courage to attempt it. In doing so, the poem serves as an uplifting message of hope and encourages those reading it to take action and never give up.
