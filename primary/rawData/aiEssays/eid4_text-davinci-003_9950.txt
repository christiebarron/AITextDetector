

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this line to show the hopeful outlook of the narrator as she looks forward to a new spring and a new beginning. She sees the test as an opportunity to prove her worth, and symbolizes this in the blossoming of the hibiscus plant, which for her reflects her own resilience and potential for growth. The line also emphasizes the importance of second chances, suggesting that difficult situations can be overcome given the right motivation and time.
