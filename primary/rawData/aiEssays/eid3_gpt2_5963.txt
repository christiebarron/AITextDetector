Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways.

The easiest way to understand the meaning of a setting is to compare that with its use in other areas of our roads.

Rough Road Ahead means that the cyclist sees the cyclist in terms of roads which are a cross-section.

A cyclist is more likely to get into the way as a result of passing through the road, especially if they see the cyclist crossing in a different manner than the way they were crossing before.
