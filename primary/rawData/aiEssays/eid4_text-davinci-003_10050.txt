

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho emphasizes the narrator's hope that through his education, he will be able to change his life. It also shows how this hope can be seen in a metaphorical way, through the hibiscus bush that he takes care of. The narrator's contemplation of the hibiscus and his hope for a brighter future makes him determined to retake his failed exam. He is willing to wait until the spring brings new growth and life, affirming his faith that opportunities will come again.
