

The ending of Winter Hibiscus implies that the narrator is looking forward to a time of renewal and a new beginning, which will be symbolized with the return of the hibiscus in the spring season. The last line is meant to symbolize the narrator's willingness to rise again after facing difficulty and hardship. It suggests that the narrator is determined to try and pass the test they failed again when the hibiscus blooms again. It is a way of showing the narrator's resilience and strength.
