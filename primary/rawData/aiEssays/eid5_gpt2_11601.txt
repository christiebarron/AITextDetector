The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' that her life has taken a turn for the worse. As a woman, she's still afraid of marriage, especially if she wants to end it.

"I haven't found my peace yet," the author writes in her memoir. "I just don't know where to turn now.

"I've made some deep decisions and there'll come a point where I feel better about what I'm doing, in life, even while I still do it on the inside. In a more comfortable way, I thought it was a pretty good idea for me to stay in my little bedroom for the day."
