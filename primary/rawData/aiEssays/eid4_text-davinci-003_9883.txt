

This ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho is a symbol of hope and perseverance. It suggests that no matter how difficult or daunting a task may seem, one can always overcome it and succeed. Even in the darkest of times, there is still hope that the future will be brighter. The hibiscus is a sign of renewal, and the idea of taking the test again in the spring symbolizes the belief that with hard work and determination, anything is possible. Furthermore, it serves to remind the reader that although life can be full of difficult and trying moments, it is important to always keep going and never give up.
