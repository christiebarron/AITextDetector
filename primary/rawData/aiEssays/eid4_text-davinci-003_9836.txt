

The protagonist is looking forward to a new start. She has experienced a difficult winter, but there is hope for the future in the coming spring. The test symbolizes her challenge, her perseverance, and her hard work in the face of the apparently insurmountable obstacle. The mention of the geese returning and the hibiscus budding represents the rebirth and revitalization that the protagonist will experience, giving her the courage to try again and to fulfill her dreams. The hopeful tone of the ending serves as an uplifting message to young readers about facing hardships and never giving up.
