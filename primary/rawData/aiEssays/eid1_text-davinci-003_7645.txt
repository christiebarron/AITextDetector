

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho implies optimism and hope that life will continue and they will have the opportunity to take the test again in the new season. It further unites the idea of the annual cycle of the passing of seasons and resurrecting hope. Spring symbolizes new beginning and the narrator's determination to take the test again paints a picture of resilience and perseverance in the face of fear and difficulty. The narrator intends to use the passing of time, of winter to spring, to motivate and remind him of his determination to take the test again.
