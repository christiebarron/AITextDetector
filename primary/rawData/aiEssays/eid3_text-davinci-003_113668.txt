The setting also creates an atmosphere of difficulty and unpredictability. The environment is rough and challenging, with road hazards to navigate and the potential for poor weather conditions to put a further strain on the cyclist. This creates an obstacle that the cyclist must endure in order to make it to the end of the race and creates a sense of tension and suspense throughout the story. 

On top of this, the setting also forces the cyclist to confront their own insecurities. The cyclist is continuously facing the fear of the unknown, as they are never sure of what will come next on the road ahead. This fear will push them to their limits, forcing them to confront difficult decisions that they may not have been prepared to make before the start of the race.
