

The ending is significant because it shows how determined the narrator is. She has faced discrimination and oppression due to her race and has put off taking a test in order to concentrate on working. However, despite all that, she is determined to face the test when spring comes and the hibiscus buds again. This symbolizes her resilience and strength and speaks to her determination to overcome her oppressive circumstances. By ending the story on this hopeful note, the author is demonstrating that hope and resilience can help one to be successful in the face of adversity.
