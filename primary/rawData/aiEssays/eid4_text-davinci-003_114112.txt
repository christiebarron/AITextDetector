

This ending effectively shows the optimism of the protagonist, in spite of thechallenges that she has faced. Winter Hibiscus is about a young girl who struggles with standardized test-taking in school due to her language barrier. She sees the way the hibiscus plant survives the winter despite the cold and snow, and is inspired to take the challenge again when the spring comes. The closing line is symbolic of this idea, and serves as a reminder that with determination and perseverance, even seemingly insurmountable obstacles can be overcome.
