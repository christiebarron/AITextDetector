

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves to provide the hopeful message that even though difficult times can seem never ending, things will eventually improve. The character is faced with the challenge of a daunting test, but instead of succumbing to fear or despair, they choose to take a proactive approach and accept that the situation may not be so grim after all. By hoping for a better outcome in the spring, the character embraces resilience and optimism, which encourages the reader to do the same.
