

Dear Editor,

I am writing to share my opinion about the effect computers have on people today. There is little doubt that computers have made an undeniable impact in the way we communicate and live our lives.

However, there is some concern among the public that computers and technology are dominating our lives to an alarming degree. It can be argued that people are becoming increasingly reliant on digital devices like computers, laptops and smart phones, which can have a detrimental effect on their mental and physical health.

From my perspective, a computer should be used wisely and responsibly. Computers allow us to access a wealth of information quickly, and enable us to stay connected with friends and family more easily than ever before. But when people spend too much time on computers, their relationships with the people around them suffer. People start to take social cues from technology, rather than from people.

On the other hand, computers can have some positive benefits. Computers can help people become more productive, organized and efficient. They can also give people access to education, employment opportunities, and online support systems.
