

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this phrase to emphasize the cyclical nature of life. She alludes to how the winter season eventually leads to the blossoming of spring, and how life continuously renews itself. In this way, she conveys the idea that failure or disappointment can ultimately lead to a renewed sense of hope and strength. By ending with this phrase, Minfong Ho seeks to comfort her reader by emphasizing the idea of renewal and the power of positivity in the face of difficulty.
