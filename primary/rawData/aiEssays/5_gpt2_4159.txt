The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' to read about her love life, where the three of them were together, and how she started out trying to understand what it took to get to where she is now.

"In her own words: 'I did what I wanted to do, you may never know what happened," she wrote.

"A year later I finally did the thing I wanted to do. And I became a mother. But I didn't like it. Some of that changed, some not."

The book was released Friday (July 26) on Kindle, and has been picked to enter the Best Female Author contest.
