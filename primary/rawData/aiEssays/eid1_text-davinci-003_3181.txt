

Dear Editor,

I'm writing to share my opinion on the effects computers have had on people.

While technology has revolutionized many aspects of our lives, the greatest impact has been in the realm of communication. People are now able to stay connected with friends, family, and business contacts from any corner of the world. By utilizing instant messaging, video conferencing, and other such methods, technology has extended our ability to communicate beyond what was ever thought possible.

However, there are some negative aspects associated with technology as well. Our reliance on computers has led to a decrease in face-to-face interaction, reducing people's ability to form meaningful connections with others. Furthermore, people have become so immersed in their online lives that they have begun to neglect their relationships in real life.

Although it is easy to become so fixated on technology that it has detrimental effects, it is important to recognize how much good technology can do. It can help us become more productive and efficient with our day-to-day activities, and it can be used as a platform to connect with other people.
