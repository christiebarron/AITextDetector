Features of the setting affect the cyclist in 'Rough Road Ahead' in many ways. The idea here is to add to the existing atmosphere by adding an awareness to how the drivers and cyclists feel in the road and whether they want to ride in a position to stop when they feel unsafe in their own riding. When the setting of Rough Road Ahead is set, you can make a statement of your'real feelings' by:

"Riding that lane if you can't stand in it and being forced to stand still. The feeling of being pulled over when you get there. Not even when everyone is coming out of their car to sit along and listen to each other in silence so the other driver does not get too close to you."

Similarly, when you set the setting of Rough Road Ahead, you can tell that this setting is a reminder that it and any other drivers or cyclists will always be at risk.
