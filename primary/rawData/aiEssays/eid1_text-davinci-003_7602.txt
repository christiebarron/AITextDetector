

The author Minfong Ho is using the hibiscus flower to act as a metaphor for her own experiences. The winter hibiscus symbolizes the challenges and setbacks she has faced in her life. By ending with this statement, Minfong Ho is expressing her hope that just as the hibiscus will bloom again in the spring, her hard work will be rewarded with success. It is also a reminder to readers to not give up hope that things that seem impossible now could still be achieved in the future.
