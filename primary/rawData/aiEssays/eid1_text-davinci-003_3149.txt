

To the Editor: 

I write to express my opinion about the impact computers have on people. In my experience, the benefits of computers far outweigh the drawbacks. Computers have changed the way we work, communicate, learn, and access information in remarkable ways, making life easier and faster than ever before.

With a few clicks of your mouse, you can search through an abundance of information and resources available from around the world. This opens up countless new job opportunities, is a great way to stay connected with family and friends, and allows people to stay on top of the latest news and trends. 

In addition, computers help to level the educational playing field, especially in lower-income areas that may not have the same resources as more affluent districts. With access to information and online classes, students are now able to learn more quickly and with higher-level resources.

Computers are not without drawbacks, such as increased sedentary lifestyles, screen time, and potential health issues. That said, if used in moderation and with some preventive measures, such as proper ergonomic posture when sitting in front of a computer, these risks can be minimized. 

In conclusion, computers have revolutionized our lives
