I believe that censorship of materials in libraries constitutes a threat to intellectual property rights, but I don't think it's a threat of the public's or anyone else's."

He added that the ACLU had "strong arguments to suggest that they are using the First Amendment to attack universities". The ACLU has been actively involved, working with local and nationwide groups to educate students about their rights, he said, "so that they are made aware of what it means to be a free person and how it can be used and abused and in particular how to protect their lives against violent and degrading forms of public harassment."

He added that universities had "no legal obligation to maintain a safe space for the general public".
