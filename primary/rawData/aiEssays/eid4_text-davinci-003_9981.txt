

The ending of this poem serves as a hopeful reminder that, even after the winter darkness and cold have passed and the days become warmer and lighter, the girl in this poem will have the opportunity to try again. The poet emphasizes that, no matter how difficult the current situation is, it is only temporary and will soon give way to new possibilities. The winter hibiscus serves as a metaphor for the difficult times the protagonist is going through right now, but it also serves as a reminder that, no matter how hard it seems right now, there will always be hope and the opportunity to try again in the future.
