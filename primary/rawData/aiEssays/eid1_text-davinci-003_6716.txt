In today’s world, computers are becoming a ubiquitous part of our lives. While it is undeniable that computers have had a significant impact on the way we work and interact with each other, there is growing concern about the potential adverse effects that these devices have on our health.

Some argue that due to the technology’s convenience, computers have made our lives easier and more productive. Yet, there are downsides to using computers too frequently. Sitting for longer than necessary and straining the eyesight from constant exposure to the computer’s light can cause physical fatigue and eye problems. People are also at risk of developing psychological issues such as depression, anxiety, and insomnia.

It is important to take into account the long-term implications of excessively using computers. Therefore, it is essential to practice healthy computer usage habits and find ways to reduce your computer time. It is also beneficial to take regular breaks to stretch or walk around and reduce the strain on your eyes.

In conclusion, computers have an undeniable impact on us, both positive and negative. In order to reduce the effect computers have on our wellbeing, it is necessary to practice healthy computer habits and be aware of the potential harm that they can cause.
