

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves as a symbolic reminder of the main character's journey of growth and change. Throughout the story, the main character has been struggling with educational pressure and feelings of inadequacy. In the final lines, the main character shows a newfound determination to not give up and take the test again when the hibiscus returns in the spring. This is a symbol of her newfound confidence and perseverance. The image of the hibiscus returning in the spring is indicative of hope, renewal, and growth and implies that the main character has made a positive change in her outlook and her outlook on life.
