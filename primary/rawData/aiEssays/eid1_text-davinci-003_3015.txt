

Dear Editor, 

I am writing to express my opinion on the effects computers have had on people. In today’s technology-driven world, computers are necessary tools that have undoubtedly improved our lives in many ways. However, I think that computers also pose certain risks that we must be aware of.

For starters, over-reliance on computers can lead to decreased social interaction. Sure, computers can allow us to stay connected with family and friends, but when used excessively they can replace real-life connections with virtual ones. Without regular face-to-face contact, our mental and emotional health can suffer.

Another potential hazard of computers is the risk of cyber-security breaches. Cybercrime is on the rise, and it’s important to be aware of how vulnerable our personal information can be.

Finally, computers can take up valuable time. Too much time spent in front of a screen can be very unhealthy, both mentally and physically. Too much sitting can lead to health problems such as obesity, heart disease, and diabetes.
