

Minfong Ho ends her poem "Winter Hibiscus" with this line to emphasize the cycle of growth, transformation, and hope that the poem has been exploring. By narrating a passage through the changing seasons, the poem suggests that there can be beauty even in the coldest times. The line serves as a reminder of the passing of time, and of the potential for a wintery landscape to come alive with newness and life. It is a reminder of resilience and of the potential for renewal.
