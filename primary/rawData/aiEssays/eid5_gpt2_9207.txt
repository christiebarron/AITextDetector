The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' is a profound reminder of how our parents have lost their independence of the home and family.

The former London mayor, Lord Rennie, said: 'It was very devastating that, as you know now at 60, I was not allowed to live in London. I was not let go. As a result the London Borough of Lewisham has now made the decision that I cannot live there or travel to other parts of England or North America.

'It has led to a decline in my relationship with his wife and family.'
