

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this line to emphasize the importance of perseverance. The speaker in the story is not successful in their attempt to pass the test, but is determined to try again when the conditions around them have changed. The changing of the season marks a new beginning for the speaker, allowing them to step out of the grey period of winter and move into the hope and renewal of spring. Through ending with this line, Ho implies that failure and setbacks are not permanent and that with enough determination and effort, even the most daunting of tasks can be achieved.
