

The speaker in the story resolves to take an important test again when the hibiscus flowers. By ending the story with this statement, the author wants to emphasize the themes of perseverance and redemption. The symbolism of the hibiscus reflects the speaker's own journey of overcoming adversity and her determination to succeed. By connecting the significance of the hibiscus to the test, the author implies that the speaker will be able to achieve her goals and find success.
