The mood created in the memoir 'From Home is' is not only to reveal the truth about one of Ireland's most brutal wars – what the Irish have done to me and the rest of their country – but to show how much Irish people have lost. One that has not only caused considerable suffering, but that in many cases has been in vain.

"This loss is part of what we have to endure and we have to fight to the death and there is a great deal that has not happened," she told the Guardian on an interview.
