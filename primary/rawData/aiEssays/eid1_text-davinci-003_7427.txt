

The ending of Winter Hibiscus conveys a message of hope and determination. The protagonist has experienced struggle, failure, and disappointment over the course of the book. The closing words represent the protagonist's commitment to take the test again in the future, when everything has thawed and new opportunities have risen. It is a declaration that the protagonist is ready to try again despite the hardships they have gone through. This ending is meant to inspire the audience, as it shows the protagonist's resilience and strength in the face of adversity.
