

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves to represent the protagonist's determination to face her challenges. Despite feeling overwhelmed and discouraged by the tests she must take to repay her father's debt, the protagonist finds hope and strength in her hibiscus plant and the winter season around her. The ending serves to highlight the protagonist's commitment to persevere and shows that her hope and faith will pay off when the snows melt and the geese return in the spring, for she will then be ready to take the test again.
