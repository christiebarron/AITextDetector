

Minfong Ho ends "Winter Hibiscus" with this line in order to imply that the protagonist has learned a valuable lesson about courage and perseverance from her little hibiscus plant. The plant is resilient and able to survive during the cold winter despite the odds, and this inspires the protagonist to face any challenge that comes her way. In the last line, she is determined to take the exam again and face the challenge boldly, just like the hibiscus plant did during the cold winter. It is a hopeful ending that implies that the protagonist will succeed in her efforts.
