

The theme of the poem is the idea of self-resilience. The speaker experiences a difficult winter that pushes him to his limits without a success yet, but he remains positive about the future. Despite his failure, he exhibits optimism that spring will bring a new chance for progress. The imagery of the blooming hibiscus is a metaphor for the Speaker being reborn in the spring, with a new opportunity to reach his dreams. By ending the poem with the imagery of the blooming hibiscus and a commitment to take the test again, the speaker is expressing the idea that you should never give up on your ambitions no matter how hard a winter it has been. Even though he may not have achieved his goal yet, he will not give up and will try again in the spring.
