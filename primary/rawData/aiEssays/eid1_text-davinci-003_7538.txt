

The ending of Winter Hibiscus serves as a reminder to readers to remain hopeful and resilient despite whatever obstacles they face. The student narrator relies on the cycle of nature to bring optimism and motivation in her life. Through the image of the hibiscus returning in spring, the narrator finds hope that she too can prevail despite her challenges and will have another chance to take the difficult test. Furthermore, the story highlights how our connections to nature can ultimately bring us courage and perseverance when we face daunting obstacles.
