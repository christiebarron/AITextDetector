

To the Editor:

I am writing to express my opinion on the effects computers have on people. Many people are of the opinion that computers are a blight on our society, distracting people from their studies or their jobs. I wholeheartedly disagree.

The undeniable truth is that computers have become an essential part of the modern world and have revolutionized the way we live our lives. By providing invaluable access to information and resources, computers have empowered us with knowledge and allowed us to stay connected with the world. With the rise of the internet, we now have access to the collective knowledge of humankind. Schools, businesses and many other entities have now adopted the use of computers in their operations, largely as a result of the benefit they offer.

Furthermore, computers facilitate an unprecedented amount of convenience. Online shopping, streaming entertainment and other modern online services let us access almost any good or service from the comfort of our homes. We can now communicate with anyone, anywhere in the world without worry or stress. All of this leads to a more productive and enjoyable lifestyle.

It's true that overuse of computers can have a negative impact in certain areas. People can become too reliant on them, and, as always, some people use the technology for nefarious purposes. Nonetheless, I believe the net benefits to using computers far outweigh the drawbacks. Not only do computers increase productivity and efficiency, but they make everyday life more convenient.

Thank you for your time,
