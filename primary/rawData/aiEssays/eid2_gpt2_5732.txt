Mr. Brown is a professor of legal studies at Washington University, where he specializes in legal and policy issues. A former judge and federal judge for the Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, he served as President George W. Bush's chief justice from 2008 to 2012. Mr. Brown is president and chief counsel to the American Freedom Law Institute.
