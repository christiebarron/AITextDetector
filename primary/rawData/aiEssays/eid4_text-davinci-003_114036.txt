The protagonist has failed the test the first time but is determined to try again and has the hope of succeeding this time when the hibiscus blooms in the spring. The symbolism of the hibiscus as a representation of hope, strength, and determination is reiterated in the closing lines. The protagonist will not give up but will continue to work towards her goal despite any obstacles or hardships that may come her way. By the end of the story, the reader is left with the feeling that despite temporary failure, the protagonist will be able to come back again and ultimately succeed.
