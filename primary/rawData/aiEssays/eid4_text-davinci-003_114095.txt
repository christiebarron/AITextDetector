

The ending of Winter Hibiscus conveys a message of hope and renewal. It suggests that no matter how difficult the future may be, that the narrator will have the strength to keep going and try again. This idea of renewal and a hopeful outlook for the future reflect the themes found throughout the poem, of resilience, perseverance, and hope. The idea that, with the return of spring and the rebirth of the hibiscus, the narrator will have the opportunity to take the test again serves as a reminder that there is always a possibility of starting anew and facing the future with courage.
