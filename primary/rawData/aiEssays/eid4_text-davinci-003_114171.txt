

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho provides a moment of hope that the narrator will be able to take the test and receive a better outcome the next time. The imagery of the snows melting and the geese returning to the area alludes to new beginnings and a fresh start and suggests that the season change will bring a renewed chance at achieving the narrator's goal. This moment of hope and the promise of a better future serves as a powerful conclusion.
