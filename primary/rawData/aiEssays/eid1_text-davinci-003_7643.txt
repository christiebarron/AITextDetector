

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho serves to motivate the main character, a first-generation immigrant named Fang, to work hard to achieve her goals. By offering a vision of hope in the future, the ending of the poem encourages Fang to keep working hard and to continue following her dreams, even when faced with challenges. The imagery of the hibiscus, which survives through the winter against all odds, symbolizes Fang's determination and strength to overcome her obstacles. The ending serves to remind Fang that her hard work will eventually be rewarded. Additionally, it serves as a reminder to the reader to stay focused and persevere, even when faced with adversity.
