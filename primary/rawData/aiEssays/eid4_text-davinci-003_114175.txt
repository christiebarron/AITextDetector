

The ending message of this poem highlights the importance of resilience. The ending emphasizes the idea that although something may be difficult and challenging, this is only temporary and it can be overcome. The narrator is referring to taking the test that they have been preparing for and although they may fail, it is important to keep trying because it will eventually lead to success. The images of spring, snow melting, and geese returning reinforces this idea of fresh starts, growth, and hope for the future. These images convey the optimism of the narrator and reflect the idea that they will be able to pass their test once they try again in the future.
