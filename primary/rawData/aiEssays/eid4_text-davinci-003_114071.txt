

The ending of Winter Hibiscus gives the story a meaningful closure by suggesting the protagonist's determination to take the test again and to not be discouraged by it. By suggesting that the protagonist will take the test again, there is a sense of hope that the protagonist can eventually achieve success. The metaphor of the hibiscus returning in the spring and the geese returning hint at a new beginning- that the protagonist is taking his efforts forward and will persist in striving for success. Further, the mention of spring conveys the idea of renewal. Thus, the ending of Winter Hibiscus is hopeful and conveys the idea of resilience.
