

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this phrase to symbolize optimism, hope, and renewal. The snows melting represents the end of the winter season and the start of a new and fresh beginning. This phrase also foreshadows the protagonist taking her test again in the future, indicating that she will eventually overcome her difficulties and doubts. Overall, the phrase serves as a way for the reader to be uplifted with the thought that adversity can be a temporary obstacle on the journey to success.
