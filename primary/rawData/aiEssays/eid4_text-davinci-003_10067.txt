

The ending is a metaphor of resilience and perseverance. The narrator is reflecting on a difficult test they failed. They then draw a parallel to the resilience of the winter hibiscus, which blooms despite the harshness of winter. This metaphor serves to inspire the narrator to not give up and to try again, when everything else around them is blooming in the spring. The narrator acknowledges that even in the harshest conditions, resilience will bring success if one perseveres long enough.
