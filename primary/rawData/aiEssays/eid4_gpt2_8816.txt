The book Winter Hibiscus ends the way it does because it is both exciting and challenging, with the promise of a comprehensive account of his journey through Celtic history that can provide a thorough grounding on the history of Celtic culture as it arose and flourished in the early centuries of European history. The book's originality alone has led to its popularity, making it introductory and challenging for students but the most comprehensive book that can be found in Europe at almost any stage of Celtic history. A critical view of all Celtic cultures is presented in the most comprehensive, up-to-date, and original way available that is still relevant today.
