

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho implies that the protagonist, Li-li, failed the college entrance exam. However, Li-li has already developed a strong love for learning and, given time and patience, will continue to work hard and reach her goals. By ending the story on an uplifting note with the protagonist's plans to take the test again, the author sends the positive message that regardless of the difficulties faced, success can be attained through hard work and determination.
