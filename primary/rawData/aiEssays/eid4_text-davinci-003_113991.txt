

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this line to emphasize the idea of hope and renewal. The hibiscus is a symbol of resilience, as it is able to survive during the harsh winter. The protagonist is also hoping to show resilience and perseverance, even as she struggles with her academic studies. The hope of the hibiscus blooming again when the snows melt speaks of renewal and of better times to come. This line reflects the protagonist's own desire to take the test again and do better, showing that even in difficult times, one can find hope in eventual success.
