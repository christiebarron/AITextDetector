

I recently moved to a new city for work, but the place I rented had more issues than I expected. My landlord kept promising to fix things, but nothing ever got done. I had to live with a broken sink, leaking roof, and broken heater. I was frustrated, but I also knew that my landlord was elderly and might be limited in his ability to fix things.

So, instead of complaining about it, I tried to go out of my way to be patient. I worked with my landlord and consulted with his family members to arrive at a solution. Eventually, we hired a contractor to take care of the essential repairs.

It was a stressful situation, but in the end, my patience paid off. The repairs were done quickly and the landlord was thankful for my understanding and patience.
