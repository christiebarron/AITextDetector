

The reason Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho ends with "When they come back in the spring, when the snows melt and the geese return and this hibiscus is budding, then I will take that test again?" is to convey hope. The girl has failed a difficult test, but she knows that she will have another chance to try again in the future. With every seasonal transition, she is given a new opportunity. In this way, the ending captures the idea of new beginnings and the optimism of change. It provides a hopeful ending for the story.
