

The author is concluding the story with an image of hope and renewal. The imagery of the snow melting, geese returning, and hibiscus budding symbolizes Spring time, which is a time of growth and opportunity. Therefore, the author is suggesting that the protagonist will use the spring as an opportunity to try again and retake the test in which she fails earlier in the story. This is a positive and inspiring message for the reader, suggesting that with each new season comes the possibility of a fresh start and the chance to try again.
