As technology continues to evolve, computers are becoming an essential part of everyday life. However, there is growing concern over the effects these devices have on people.

My opinion is that computers have had a positive impact on society. Additionally, computers allow us to work remotely, which can have a great impact on personal and professional growth.

One concern is that computers can hinder our social lives; since individuals often prefer to stay in their homes and interact online instead of spending time with family and friends. Moreover, studies prove that computer usage at a young age can lead to physical and mental health issues. Therefore, it is important to ensure that exposure to computers is monitored.

In conclusion, computers have had a tremendous and unprecedented impact on society. If used responsibly and with moderation, computers can revolutionize the way we learn, work, and interact.
