

The ending of Winter Hibiscus by Minfong Ho symbolizes hope and resilience. The narrator is facing a difficult test, and they've been struggling with it. They draw inspiration from the winter hibiscus that continues to persist through the harsh winter, despite its likelihood of dying. This gives the narrator hope that they too can persevere through their difficulty and take the test again when the snows melt and the geese return. The ending conveys the message that no matter how hard the situation, there is light at the end of the tunnel, and that with enough resilience, one can make it through.
