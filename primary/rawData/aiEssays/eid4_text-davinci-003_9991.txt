

Minfong Ho ends Winter Hibiscus with this hopeful tone of the speaker returning to take the test again in the spring. By focusing on the seasonal cycle of the hibiscus, Ho emphasizes that a re-testing could mean a new winter hibiscus and better chances at the speaker succeeding. Furthermore, the imagery of the snows melting, the geese returning, and the hibiscus budding act as metaphors of renewal and hope. By conveying optimism and leveraging the imagery of nature, Ho ends on an uplifting note for the speaker.
