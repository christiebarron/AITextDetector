

I remember the day my son got his first tooth. We had been waiting for it to come in for quite some time. He was teething and had been cranky and uncomfortable for what felt like forever. It was a frustrating situation because nothing seemed to be helping him. I had to be patient as I saw him in pain and could not do anything to make it stop.

It wasn't easy and I could feel my patience slipping away as the days went by. But eventually, after what felt like an eternity, his first tooth finally arrived. Seeing my son's smile made it all worth it. I had persevered and stayed patient under a difficult situation and it paid off in the end.
